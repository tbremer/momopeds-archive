Re: Exhaust port opening or not
« Reply #7 on: May 31, 2010, 08:57:22 am »
Sounds like maybe you need to gear a little higher? If you've got THAT much low end, then I'd turn it into some top end. With that carburetor, you're probably looking at 50 tops. The stock port map on those cylinders really restricts the top rpm; which is why they're so torquey from the factory. I used a thick paper gasket, I eventually tried using two, and got the best results then. This was before I milled it. I took about 2mm off the top, then made a brass base gasket to space it up. My port timings were pretty high. Which pipe are you running? Get a bigger carb, bump your exhaust port up a couple mm, tune, and enjoy 55.
