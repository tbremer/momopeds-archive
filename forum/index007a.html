The time has come, and it's a big task. I am hoping for no more than 24 hours of downtime, but I can't predict the future.

My plan is two step:
   1. Move all the content
   2. Move all the databases

For the content, you all shouldn't see anything change. It might be a little slower since I will be migrating 7ish GB of data, but no interruptions in service.

For the databases, I am going to put the forum in maintenance mode to block all postings and save the DB structure, move it over to the new server, upload, and check for any lost data. And firing everything back up. -- This is the part I am more fearful of something going awry. However, I have 90% confidence (and some documentation) that tells me it will all go smoothly.
