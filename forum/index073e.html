General Discussion / SF moped shops
« on: June 06, 2009, 11:24:59 am »
Yesterday after checking out Alcatraz I made my way down to the Mission neighborhood where Treats and 1977 are located. It's pretty awesome because they are located in the latino neighborhood so there are taquerias everywhere!

First I went to treats which is basically like a shotgun storage unit in a row of shops. Benji, Jimmy (the guy who does all the shipping) and Roofie (spelling?) were there. They were all super nice. Jimmy told Benji he ships to STL everyday! So Benji gave me 3 spark plug wrenches and a header for my exhaust as a gift since I came all the way from the Lou. He also gave me a discount on the clutch bell housing I picked up for Phil.

I gave the guys one of our little cards. They thought it was rad and Benji actually asked Roofie to scan it in for him. Benji wanted me to tell everyone thanks so much for the support and he says the better business is (the more we buy) the lower the prices can go. There were some guys there from San Jose as well who gave me a copy of the Drunken Moped zine which I'll share with you all. I took pics which I'll post when I get back in town.

Next I made my way to 1977 after going 10 blocks the wrong way on the wrong street! I got there and got an awesome shirt. I also gave them one of our cards and they thought it was rad too. They invited me to ride with them Monday night but I'll be back in STL then.

Everyone I met moped related was really cool. One guy at 1977 seemed interested in coming to STL. I told him about the fall rally. Benji also said they have a place for people to crash too when they are in town so if anyone comes out this way let him know.

I've only spotted one pair of mopeds out side of the ones I saw at the shops. It was a maxi and a pugoet (spelling?). Lots of scooters here though.

Pics to come. For now I'm off to Muir Woods.

Hope you guys in KC are having fun.
