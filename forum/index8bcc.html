..also, cutting the piston crown will effectively raise your exhaust port the same amount that was cut off. I think you'd be way better off just raising the cylinder, and milling quite a lot off the top, then rechecking port timings. Since it's a cast cylinder, you wouldn't have to worry about nikasil flaking off.
