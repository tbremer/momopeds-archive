Hey everyone!

So, I'm taking a studio lighting class and I had to come up with some sort of final project that has to deal with controlled lighting to work on for the next month. I decided that I want to recreate vintage moped ads from the 70s/80s but with some sort of modern twist on them. I'm posting this on mopedstl because I need people to photograph! If you're free and relatively close to Lawrence in these next 3-4 weeks I would love to come out and take a picture of you with one of your mopeds. If you live in Lawrence or are willing to drive to Lawrence, I'll plan studio time to shoot, otherwise I'll drive to you and we can do on-location shots. I need atleast 10 people but I would like to take as many portraits/ads as I can, so the more the merrier! Also, if you help me out I'd be happy to take some extra pictures of just your moped if you want a nice picture of it.
