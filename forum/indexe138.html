No you gotta buy a few jets and do it yourself. You'll probably be in the 60's range. I think the 14.12 comes with a 53 or a 57 I can't remember.

Just buy a 5 pack of jets like this: //www.treatland.tv/dellorto-main-jets-60s-range-p/dellorto-main-jets-range-60s.htm Then tune your bike. Start large and work your way down until it doesn't four-stroke. Then do some plug chops. You can always pull your air filter to lean it out, or add the filter to make it richer while you're waiting for the right jet to come in the mail after your initial purchase.

The 14.12 is a great choice! You don't have to tune the needle like a bing, which can make them problematic for some setups but also incredible easy to set up. Don't worry about it facing the frame either, you'll tune the idle adjuster once and never touch it again. It takes 30 seconds I promise
