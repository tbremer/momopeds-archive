I made it home last night.  Amazing since I was missing 1 rear axle nut (one side just held on by chain adjuster), and apparantly all but 3 of the bolts holding my rear sprocket on my rear wheel (there should be 10 I think).  Those three weren't spaced out, they were all right next to eachother, so the sprocket flops around everytime the wheel goes around.  But hell, I made it home, and raced some mexican dudes in a civic on the way.  They beat me off the line, but I caught them right quick.

See, when I first kitted my magnum I was real good about checking my nuts.  Giving them a good carress now and then.  But they never moved.  All of a sudden my nuts are disappearing.  My magnum is going to be called Hitler from now on.  Or maybe Lance Armstrong...that would be less controversial.  But it is a german ped...

Remember: pay more attention to the nuts.
