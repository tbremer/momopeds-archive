'Smash and grab' moped gang jailed

One of Britain?s most prolific ?smash and grab? moped gangs, who stole millions of pounds worth of goods in more than 70 raids at exclusive London stores, has been jailed.

By Richard Edwards, Crime Correspondent

Six members of the gang were sentenced to a total of 22 years for a ?shock and awe? robbery on a jewellers in Cockfosters, north London.

Police sources said the gang are linked to dozens of other raids in the West End in the past two years at stores including Harvey Nichols, Selfridges, Watches of Switzerland, Mappin & Webb and Ernest Jones.

They stole more than ?4 million of goods in the past year alone, which are then sold on the black market.

Since the arrest of the men in June, the number of raids has dropped in central London by 95 per cent.

The offenders were part of a large loose-knit gang who used stolen high-powered mopeds or motorbikes to stage the two minute ?smash and grab? raids.
