I finally got my magnum running yesterday.  I still needs some stuff done to it, and to get broken in, but it is pretty sickly fast.  It doesn't have any low end...until the clutch engages, then you gotta hold on.

I'm getting a GPS in the mail anytime now.  Once I get it on there and know how fast I'm actually going, I'll let yous know.

Oh, the setup is nothing crazy.  Actually, it's a "mild" kit setup:
65cc metrakit
15mm bing
matching 15mm intake
Techno boss pipe
dremeled out the transfers in the case to match the kit
stock everything else, and I'm running it real rich for break in
