it's  a 2005 tomos streetmate, airsal kit milled head and ported piston 19mm phbg knockoff dellorto and technigas next pipe. geared low so me and my girl can both cruise a steady 40mph. geared high it rips. the frame was cut down to line up the homemade seat with the tank. the tanks from a harley sportster and i had to put the crossbar in because the stock bike uses the frame as the tank. front fender is from a kromag and i cut it up and made stainless brackets to fit it. rear fender from a harley as well. i made the battery box and chainguard out of stainless using a ball peen hammer and vise grips. the handlebars are from a mountainbike. the hardest part was hiding the wiring harness, this thing has a ridiculous amount of wire inside the fake gas tank/fairing thing on a stock streetmate. also it has the human soul of some unfortunate stranger. it was the object of a voodoo curse once.
