Re: WinteR ProjecT
« Reply #6 on: January 16, 2011, 10:29:37 pm »
It handles great! I though t would need to use diff forks but they work great. There are two nuts at the bottom side of the fork that's for mounting the fender, if you leave them bolts out the fork springs are really lose but if you tighten them up real tight, the forks become more rigid. They feel just as rigid as any stock moped forks. I may do one tiny gas strut on each side later on, the same ones you find on a hatch back to hold the hatch open on a car. I heard you can mount one to each side and it eliminates the bounce.

I will also mention if some one is heavy they would be better off going with diff forks but for some one really light these forks feel great.
