My ride computer used to get crazy readings, and then it would freeze up like windows 98!
 After a little research I found that my 1977 spark plug was Not a resister plug!.
so my bikes electrical  system was zapping everything electronic 
It's an electrical disturbance called "back EMF"!
It used to get a loud BUZZZZZ on my digital camera recordings, that would change pitch with engine RPM.
switching to a modern plug solved the problem (there has to be an "R" somewhere in the plug number)

 I made a "c-mount" for that camera, the intense vibration of the hard mounted engine damaged the camera.
