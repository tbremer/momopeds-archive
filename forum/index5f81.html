No this was a black magnum kx stock pipe.
Cut down some what, with the tail section of a Chinese 4-stroke pocket bike pipe stuck on the back.
the angles of the thing are "close", we used the measurements from a proma circuit pipe (off a hobbit and straightened) we had to "add" some length to the belly section to get the timing within spec for the 50cc jug.

that "boss" is next . speaking of which did you know that the length of that "boss" pipe indicates that it won't "Hit" until almost 20,000 rpm?
that's why its so cheap, because it "looks like an expansion pipe" but its really worthless!

it's a Dellorto clone 15.5mm, the intake is bored to 17mm. this is a low-buck performance mod, we have about $40 in this round of tuning. I would like to see 45mph with a little more fine tuning, but like I said before. it's doing a good 34mph on GPS with no real "by the book" tuning!
