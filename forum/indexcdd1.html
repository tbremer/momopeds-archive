wiscpeds rally report and feeler.
« on: November 06, 2011, 10:33:34 pm »
well the lil madi mini rally of impromptuity was pretty fun! some hnrs, cranks, and great local riders were there! they have some badass road ways to ride. we rocked a graveyard saturday night and rode this wonderful sorta back woods esk road that was through the lake park in the middle of madison. we even we're given the bird at least twice by a old man cyclist and a power walking old lady.

in the pics you'll see chris's pink maxi he got thrown together for the rally. saturday night his baffle blew off. he rode it with out sunday which may have been why we got the bird from the old people.

dig jimmy crank's awesome red freespirit. dig that pipe. it's a blast!

this yellow garelli was my favorite. it had a polini and one of the first batches of pipes by mlm. as zach would say, it was a big boy moped. after riding it i realized how conservative the dude who was riding it rode. cuz it would blast ass.

i got a few more pics i'll post up layter for people to look at when yalls bored.


it was fun, and yall should check what you want to do for new years. lots of us went to colo to ride cold. how about up here this time? the cranks would be down to host some fun in milwaukee when i asked. would anybody be interested in coming up for new years?
