Re: Bad Vespa HT coil?
« Reply #6 on: July 29, 2011, 11:46:58 pm »
It turned out that the coil is bad. After doing some research I discovered that the primary and secondary portions of the coil can be checked for resistance level with an ohm meter. Can't remember which one is which, but one has resistance of about 10,000 ohms and the other is around 1,000 ohms. When checking mine I found that either the primary or secondary had infinite resistance. Replaced the coil and everything is now fine. The lesson learned is if you see arcing at the coil between the coil and another part of the bike it is probably a bad coil.
