Actually the 4t carbs tend to work okay on a 2t reed valve. They have different fueling requirements than piston port motors. I've got a customer running one of the VM18s on a ported Polini and it does fine. If you already have one it's totally worth a try, especially if it works with the stock intake. Obviously you'd have to open up the stock intake (12mm) if it bolts on.
