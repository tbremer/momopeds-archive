Not to be a Debbie Downer, but that crank is first and foremost. Either blow an obscure crank, or crack that engine and rebuild it somehow. I remember taking off my Derbi clutch and finding a wobbly crank...a sad day indeed.  I had mine sent to Ed Exline (Mopagan on MA) for peace of mind and critical evaluation. That Dude is a Wiz Bang, and he does AMAZING work, but I don't know if he rebuilds 'em regularly. Anyhow, a rebuild is something to consider. I wouldn't throw much into that bike until that issue gets addressed in my opinion. Though, to be optimistic, there is a lot of information nowadays on how to rebuild it yourself. My two cents.
