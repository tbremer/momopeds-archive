zucchini casserole:
4 c grated zucchini
1 cup Bisquick

1/4 c veg. oil
1/2 c parm cheese
4 eggs
1 med onion - diced small
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
1 tsp parsley

combine zucchini & Bisquick
combine remaining engredients. pour liquid stuff over zucchini and toss together.

Pour all into greased 9x9 pan. cook  @ 350 for 25-30 minutes or till brown on top.

I doubled all this stuff for the pan last night... also tossed ina little mozzerella cheese too.
