A funny (NOT!) thing happened to me on my way home tonight from a group ride with the Holy Rollers (Ofallon, Mo.)  We had a great ride as always with this group, lower temps than what we've been having, just all around good time.
I use an enclosed trailer to haul my bikes around cause they dont fit in my van very well (conversion, not much room).  So I'm entering I-70 I just got on the highway and I heard a noise that I never heard pulling this trailer before, I look in the mirror and the trailer is shaking a bit so I slow down just a tad and I look in the mirror again now it's jumping and swaying side to side so I slow more.  I look in my mirror again and the trailer isnt there hahahahahahaha  I see sparks shooting in the air on the shoulder  and then I see the trailer go on the grassy embankment about 30 ft. along side of me and now it's passing me... I thought OH SHIT!!! it's gonna roll and my 2 bikes in there will be destroyed!!!!!!!  So Im slowing down more to watch it and it starts comming back at me headed toward the highway, so I positioned my van to block it from getting back on the road and then the front of it hit a sign at the shoulder and it stopped OMG!!!!!!!!!   So I call MaddMatt and ask him to get out here to help me
 ( he lives close) before the cops show up, it's dark I dont have a flashlight blah blah and I'm freakin out big time  I really dont know what the F--- to do.  So he's on his way I get out to see what damage is done and what I can do....   and what happened to cause this shit.   
The reciever was still on the trailer hitch and the safety chain was off.   There was a group of teenagers gathering where my trailer was for the day/night, sooo"s  I'm thinking that the teenage boys must have thought it would be funny to take the the clip that holds the reciever pin on off and they took the safety chain off as well hhahhahahaha funny huh
Matt was smart enough to bring a pin and clip from his trailer  so we were able to put it back on and presto!!! on the road again.
   The trailer ended up without much damage a dent from hitting the sign, the bikes were still strapped down and were ok.  My frickin nerves are shot and my remedy is writing this and drinking whiskey at the moment.  From now on I will be checking my hookup when being left alone for sure AMEN!!!!!!   HOLY SHIT!!  more whiskey please!!!!
