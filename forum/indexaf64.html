« on: October 20, 2008, 02:32:43 pm »
//www.examiner.com/r-2938212~Hit_and_Run_Injures_Two_Men_on_Moped.html

Lexington County (WLTX) - State troopers say they're looking for the people who they say hit a two people on a moped, then drove away from the scene.

The collision took place on Highway 6 near Bethany Chuch Road in Lexington County around 2 a.m. Friday.

The circumstances leading up to the event aren't yet know, but it did result in two men being knocked off of the vehicle. Both were taken to the hospital. Their exact condition is not known.

Troopers are looking for a vehicle of interest that may know something about the accident. The vehicle is a dark color Chrysler PT Crusier. Troopers say the vehicle will have damage on the driver's side and driver's side mirror. The PT Cruiser was seen leaving Kent's Corner Exxon at Highay 6 and Highway 302 a short time after the moped. The collision occured a short distance from the store.

Anyone who may have been in the area at the time of the collision or may know who was involved is asked to call Crimestoppers anonymously at 1-888-CRIME-SC.
