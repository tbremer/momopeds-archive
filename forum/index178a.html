Fair enough.

I'll fill you all in on the issue I am having and maybe someone will have awesome advice.

My bike:
Cimatti City Bike
75cc Polini kit
19mm PHBG carb
Mamba pipe

Runs great, lots of torque, but when I get to high RPM I get a loud bang sound which I am assuming is back fire. The plug looks perfect, so I don't think it is too rich.

Suggestions other people have had:
- Too rich if the backfire is in the exhaust. I have checked the plug and if anything it may be lean so I don't think this is the issue, plus I have tried down jetting significantly and get the same result.
- Too lean if the backfire is in the carb. Again, plug looks good.
- Timing is off. We checked this with a timing light last night and got it to a good spot. Still back fire. More can probably be done here (as in, retarding the timing further, cleaning the points, etc...)
- Condenser is bad. This would be resolved with the CDI I believe.
- Coil/spark cable from coil is bad.
- Fuel not flowing well. I will get a new filter as they are cheap, but fuel seems to be flowing fine.

I am very open to suggestions. Thanks in advance.
