I agree with the acid.  Muriatic acid will be the easiest to get ahold of.  The amount of rust you have will determine how much acid you need.  For a four liter tank, add 400 mL of muriatic acid and fill the rest with water.  This will make ~5% acid solution.  At this concentration your aluminum pieces will be fine (ditch the seal on the cap, or see if it holds up, i dont know).  Fill the tank and let it sit for a couple hrs.  Shake it every once in a while.  Drain the tank into a bucket and neutralize the acid with baking soda until it stops fizzing.
