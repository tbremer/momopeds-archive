Take the ring off the piston carefully. More carefullier! Then stick it in the cylinder (I go about halfway down toward the ports) and measure the gap with your set of feeler gauges (less than 5 bucks at advanced auto, oreilly, etc). If it is smaller than spec, take it out and lightly file the gap and repeat until it is the right size.
