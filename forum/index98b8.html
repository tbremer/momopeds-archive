Performance springs dont go on the starter clutch. The springs on the starter clutch are fairly light tension.-not sure if thats what you did

There are performance springs that work on the reugular US clutch, typically people run lighter variator weights and a heavy contra spring and stock clutch springs.  The light weights and heavy contra spring keep the variator from variating at lower rpms helping you acelerate quicker, not as much need to tune the clutch as on a non varated bike but you can depending on what you want the bike to do.
