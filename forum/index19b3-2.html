Nothing from the Chetak is gonna fit the Allstate frame, sorry. The 4T Bajaj engine doesn't swap to Vespas, and the Fork will only fit P-series Vespas, unless you make some serious hacking and welding modifications. The Cowls won't fit any Vespa, and certaintly not an allstate, without hacking and welding either. :-(

Bajaj is from India. They made Vespas for Vespa, until Vespa left India. Then, as part of the deal, Bajaj kept making the Vespas they had been making for Vespa, excpet now they were Bajajes. That "clone" body style lasted till about the 80's(i think). In the meantime, Bajaj took what they learned from Vespa, and made many Vespa-like machines, but designed by thier own engineers. The 4T Chetak, is one of those machines. Almost nothing swaps over to Vespas anymore from the newer Bajaj scooters. (rims, maybe the seat, maybe the glovebox but that's about it)

If you have any questions, or want to double check, ask just about any Toto, they'll know. But tell them I got first dibs on the fork. :-p
