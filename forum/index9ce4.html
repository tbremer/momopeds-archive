Title: Re: Grande speedo drive & cable
Post by: EuGene on June 07, 2010, 06:49:02 pm
When you hold it in your right hand with the tab (that turns with the wheel) sticking out towards the left and turn it in the same manner the wheel would turn it, look down in the hole where the cable plugs in and see which direction the square cable socket turns.  If it is turning clockwise, then it is the correct drive thingy.

If you can't see down in the dark hole to tell which way it is turning then try this:

Hold it in front of you with the part where the cable is inserted pointing upwards and the side that would face the brake drum pointed away from you, then

if it looks like this -----> d  it will turn counter-clockwise and is the wrong one

if it looks like this -----> b  it will turn clockwise and is the correct one!! :)
