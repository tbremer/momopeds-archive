I disagree, I think temp gauges are a MUST have... I can't tell you how many times I would've seized my bikes when I had the HPI on them... just a LITTLE too advanced and it would make the temps jump 20-40degs, maybe more... I just timed my bike with the treats CDI i'm at 1.1mm BTDC and holding steady WOT for hella days and only hitting 300-320deg.  At 1.5mm (which is about full advance on the treats CDI) I was seeing 370deg... i'm getting loads more power at 1.1mm (it IS a polini however, i've never had to set timing at 1.1mm BTDC with anything else) then I was at full advance... just sayin'... temp gauge is a must.
