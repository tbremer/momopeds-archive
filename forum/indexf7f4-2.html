I was going to get the DMP Stuffed (bearing party pack), and unstuff it; but now that I've really checked out the stock one, I dunno. How well do they hold up?

So, the big question is..
Keep the stock crank, or go for the DMP Stuffed and unstuff it?

Good choice, where do I send them?

By the way, when your hauling ass (like top speed down a hill) and U let off the throttle (like its completely closed) do you here a clattering-

ringing sound from your engine?

If you don't know what this sound is like, just say "I don't know", If you do you may have a bearing issue.

If you have your engine apart, clean off all the oil from your piston pin, and the inside of your bearing.

Then place the pin in to the bearing, and try to pull the rod back and forth(not side to side).

Your looking for "play" that you can feel, but probably don't have the tools to measure. It feels like a very subtle taping, allmost unnoticeable.

Also you can check for twisting at the pin (not like round and round, but front to back)
