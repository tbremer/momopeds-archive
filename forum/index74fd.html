Title: Re: new guy.
Post by: FakeDad on May 24, 2011, 09:29:46 am
Well these old oil injectors eventually fail, and they do so without warning, then the bike seizes hard and then the good times stop rolling. The oil pump unit also gets in the way of having easy carb access. And when you take out the oil tank you can stick the battery under the seat, it opens up the lines of the bike a bit. Disabling the oil injector is something you'll have to do once you start making performance mods, as the pump won't keep the proper ratio.

 The proma circuit is a great pipe on a either a stock or kitted set-up. You'll have to do some bracket modding and lose the center stand, but it levels the hills for ya, blasty blasty 40ish.
