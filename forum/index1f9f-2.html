Title: Re: Breakin'
Post by: Mike Pee on May 13, 2009, 02:56:53 pm
I've got a shit ton of miles on my 65 metrakit.  You need to hit all the RPM ranges, but none of them for too long.  So just vary your speed a lot.  Don't keep your throttle in the same position forever, especially not wide open.

The first like 30-50 miles are real important to treat all proper-like.

The next 250 or so are the end of break in.

After that, beat it till it dies.  But it's a metra, it probably won't die unless you do something dumb.  And even then you can get a new piston and bore it out.
