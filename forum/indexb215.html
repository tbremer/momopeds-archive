Sent you some information - but misread your initial symptoms.  If you have spark and no fuel on a 1987 Volvo, the problem is most likely a bad fuel pump relay. 

Fuel but no spark is probably a bad FI fuse. 

If you need some in-person TLC I can fine some time.  However, rule out the obvious/common failures first.
