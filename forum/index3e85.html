Title: Re: just bought a pedal-less moped
Post by: Mike Pee on October 13, 2008, 09:42:23 pm
If you do pull the head and cylinder, know that the rings on the piston have a gap that has to fit around a pin in each groove when you put it back together.  If you look, you'll know what I'm talking about.  If they don't line up, you'll break your ring(s) when you try to put it back together.  It's annoying.
