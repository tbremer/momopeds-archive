Post by: Mike Pee on July 16, 2008, 12:13:28 pm
Barely functions is the key.  No one likes the people with them fancy bikes that can stay running for more than 2 trips without tinkering.  Except Dave.  We all love Dave because he has detailed knowledge of magic, nuclear missiles, anything that uses electricity, and engines, of course.  Things that stump 8 of us for weeks Dave can fix in 30 seconds.

Like last night, when he got Annie's express headlight working after me, Annie, and Mark tried for like 3 days straight and failed at every juncture.  Dave wiggled his magical fingers, and a lightning bolt flew in from the clear blue sky.  We thought the express would be fried, but when we checked, the lightning had perfectly soldered the right wires together and the headlight worked once more.  Then he whistled a tune and squirrels, rabbits, and small birds found all the right screws to put the headlight bucket back together.  Then Dave pronounced that his work was complete.  He threw down a smoke bomb and disappeared.  Though we could hear the zipping of a 2-stroke in the distance...  Amazing.
