rat-le can that shit! the spray paint that's made now days is way better than the shit that gave it a bad name. it's all in the prep work,  dry time, and patience. wipe it down real good with denatured alcohol, sand every square inch with 220, wipe it down really, really good with the alcohol again, keep everything super clean. hang it up and hose it down with 2 coats of your favorite flavor. put 2 coats of clear engine enamel on it. it's recommended that 70 degrees is the drying temperature, so take it inside and hang it up, and forget about it for a week.
