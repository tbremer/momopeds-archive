 i get a vision of what i gotta do, then see if i have the shit laying around to make it happen. as i select the material for the project, i take into consideration if it's too light or heavy. of course it can be shaped or shortened or lengthed, but will it hold up. so, there's the material
 i wanna make it as small as possible, knowing that the weakest part is stronger than the strongest part of the part i'm replacing.
other than that, make it look cool. i look at things i like, bikes, guitars, guns, motors, etc..
keep it clean, plan on doing as little as possible on the clean up, and keep that in mind through the whole process.
it took 7 days to cut these pieces out. it took 6 months to figure out how i was gonna go about making the pieces.
to be continued....work:(
