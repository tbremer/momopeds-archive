It depends on the forks! and the caliper set up.

Mike Thomas did say that he prefers his set up with the EBR disc forks, because they're easier to adapt / make a spacer.
(http://exposefortheshadows.com/random/Screen%20Shot%202011-09-02%20at%2010.35.47%20AM.png)

However, really, all you have to do is space your wheel to the center and make an appropriate adapter for your disc/caliper setup. Nice thick steel or aluminum cutdown and spaced right should do fine.

I am in process of doing the same thing, disc brakes on pit bike forks with 5-stars using Mike Thomas set up. I should have everything in a couple of weeks, just waiting on the new disc setups to come out.
