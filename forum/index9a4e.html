1)Unscrew and remove the screen on the new petcock. 2)Install the screen most of the way into the frame. 3)Re-install the screen by threading it back into the petcock. 4)Install the nut onto the frame and the petcock and tighten it down. One thread is reversed so they will both tighten at the same time 4)Hook up your brand new fuel line to your new petcock and fully rebuilt carb. 5) Fill the tank with gas and ride... New petcock settings should be up=OFF middle=ON down=reserve   Make sure and check these settings to be sure.
