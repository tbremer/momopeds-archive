My only interest in MGBs is that the community is HUGE.  Between the community and several specialty suppliers (Moss Motors and Victoria British) you can rebuild your entire car. 
Old Volvos are great, but it took me years of research to find sufficient sources and folks to get the rebuild parts.  Many are very pricey, and some things are simply not available. 

And of course.. there's no vintage volvo convertible (except the uber-rare P1900 and a few custom cars)



---

I'd recommend a 240 Volvo.  Actually... there's a guy down in Springfield with a 1974 142 (4-door).  The 140 was the the first Rock-Solid Volvo made - stronger than the 1800 and the Amazon - forerunner of the venerable 240.  Had some roof damage, but looks pristine otherwise.  ~$2500 and he's interested in trades.  It'd hold your family too.
