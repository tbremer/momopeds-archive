An hs-50 is one of the bikes I would love to buy and hold onto forever.

They are amazing looking, I assume fun to ride (seeing as they're built like the other Bats I have ridden), crazy stable and easy to find parts for (though more expensive than Puchs).

On a down note they have a somewhat hard to work with clutch (pain to assemble) and after 25-30 years they tend to seize up until you give them a good work over.
