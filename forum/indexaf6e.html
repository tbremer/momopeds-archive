The crush gaskets from treats are too small and restrict your exhaust flow when crushed. I go to ACE hardware and buy little copper tubes for 2 bucks, bend them around an appropriate sized socket, and cut and solder them. It only takes a few minutes and they are the right size for the header on the weak-ends and MLM pipes. I have never rocked a proma but I would make them for it anyway since it costs ~$0.65 to make one vs the cost of one + shipping online.
