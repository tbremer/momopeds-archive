sounds like your timing slipped.

which will happen when you don't use a woodruff key

so to reset it find TDC, then set the piston @ 1.6mm BTDC --- do this with the cam pulled off but by you placing slight pressure on it until you find the fire point.  Once you have the piston at 1.6mm BTDC carefully pull the cam off and zip tie it to the flywheel (that was a good idea) place the whole she-bang on to the crank, making sure the "F" mark on the flywheel lines up with the fire mark on the case.  Tighten everything down and cruise.  Oh yeah, cut the zip ties off before you ride.
