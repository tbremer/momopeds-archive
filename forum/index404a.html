That chopper belongs to one of the members of the Solex Club Spirales from Calais, France. There are several choppers and other weird Solexes in the group. These people are extremely cool...I met many of them when I was in France. Calais is very close to England and most of the Calais people speak English very well, and have a sort of connection to England and America. When I was in France, one of the Spirales' choppers was flying a huge American flag. A lot of people thought it was my Solex.
