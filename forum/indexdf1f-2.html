thought I'd share that little reminder. I met a few of you at last months vintage bike night, I was on a stock qt50. Since then I've got a 2001 tomos sprint that needed some help. It had a 70cc puch style kit (no reeds, a3 style exhaust angle) arreche 15mm carb, bi-turbo pipe, puch hi-comp head. kit was used up, leaking out of head gasket, worn out piston and cylinder. So I got a a35 70cc kit, reeds, fabbed up a intake, bent the header to fit the a35 angle, new bars, etc. got it tuned right and running great. 42mph (according to iphone app not speedo) with me on it (240lbs!)



rode it everywhere, put over 500 miles on it in a couple weeks. Sunday I was riding down hampton and the perfect storm of traffic + a loose dog in the street and long story short I rear ended a car at about 30mph. other than a sick leg burn in the shape of head fins and some road rash I am fine I was wearing a full face helmet. pretty scary crash though, heres the bike now:

new forks soon, and i'll be back out there...
