saga of the ratty mb5s
« on: May 27, 2011, 09:51:26 am »
Not a mopad, but a handful of us have them so I thought I'd postify.

I got an MB5 a couple years ago, thought it had issues, and parted it out, but still had a bunch of shit laying around.
Last year Tom, Loren, and Jimmy got MB5s, so I kept my eye out so I could be a cool kid too.  Found a super shitty one for $140 then ignored it for like...10 months.  So the other night I took a coffee mug, filled it half with bourbon and half with coffee, and got to work.

The $140 mb5 was a parts bike at a shop, then a parts bike for another dood.  No biggie, got the shit I need to make it live.  Well, someone took all the bearings out of the fork, then re-tightened everything down as if it were fine.  When they did this, the bottom bearing cup got smooshed up and inside the headtube at a fucked angle.  I tried to pound it back right, but no luck.

So I spent the next 5 hours swapping everything to a bare frame.  Forks mounted right, swingarm, engine, exhaust, etc all there.  But I gots no wires.  This weekend I'm gonna tackle the rewiring and trying to get this thing together.  Gonna be rattastic.
