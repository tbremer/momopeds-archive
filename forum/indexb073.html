I don't have a job and want money, but I also want a moped soooo...

$1500 takes it.

or $1000 and a nice pinto/free spirit/magnum

Derbi Revolution w/Start 5 (kick start flat reed) motor:

* Rebuilt bottom end with Nachi/SKF bearings
* Rebuilt crank from Mr. Crankshaft + new malossi small end bearing
* Metrakit cylinder w/piston that I cut boost ports into to match the cylinder
* Motomatic race pipe
* Lomat intake w/vforce reeds and tm24 carb
* Lusito throttle and right hand brake lever (front brake)
* Stock Pirelli tires still in good shape
* Polini variator
* 2k contra spring
* Artek K2 clutch with white "sport" clutch springs and "cooling hole" cut into kickstart cover to cool the stock clutch bell down

Notes:

* A small chunk of rear fender is missing from my buddy falling off his board and it hitting the fender
* I removed the front headlight w/fairing (and sold it) and made some little brackets to use a normal CEV headlight. Makes things much simpler, and it kind of looks like a Ciao now.
* All plastics besides the front fairing come with it, except the oil tank cover, I don't have that one anymore.

What it needs:

* Nothing, or an HPI I guess. But you'd have to modify it to fit.
