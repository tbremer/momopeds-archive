Now that I stop and think... I've seen caps do this when they get physically deformed. But yeah, they usually die open.

Based on what Troy said, the prob is either a ground or open circuit somewhere in the ignition. Chasing wires is the only way to find it. Oh and make sure that yr brake-light circuit isn't jank, on V1s that circuit is the ground for the ignition coil. You could test that real quick by grounding the blue wire (usually) coming out of yr stator and try then test for spark. If that circuit is open anywhere you will never see spark. If you switched out brake-light switches recently, beware, Puch ones will not work. They are open when yr not on the brake. The Italian bikes tend to be the opposite, they are closed normally.
