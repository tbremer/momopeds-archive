tonight in a van down by the river (party /punk rock / good ride content)
« on: November 06, 2011, 04:28:00 am »
guerrilla / generator show going on tonight down by the river. the bands will be playing literally inside a van (1989 ford clubwagon, specifically). theres some talk of bonfires, BYOB. show up at 6. i will be riding my moped from overland. gonna be fun as hell. moped folk should ride out!

here's the directions:

Either ride your bike about three miles North from the head of the Riverfront Trail just North of down town or by car, take I 70 to the Adelaide exit and head east on Adelaide ( turn right if you are coming from the city.) Make a right on Hall St. then make your first left. The road has no proper road sign but there is a large sign that says Buckeye Terminal. Follow this road as it turns to the right then go left and over the tracks. You will go through an opening in the wall and make a right on to the trail. Take it down just past two buildings and park there. You should see a van and hopefully some fucking people.

this is the exact location, on mapquest: //mapq.st/vNO3ET
