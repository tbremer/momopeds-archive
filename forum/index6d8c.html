Here are the specs on that Moby. If you go to the site on the bottom of that pic you can see more pics of the engine and whatnot.

1975 French 125 Inter Champion (Rider Michel Baloche)

This bike is an authentic official factory GP bike, run by the official Motob?cane team in the 1975 Inter French Championship and in some of World championship events. The machine is complete and 100 % original and has been confirmed by its rider Michel Baloche after an inspection in August 2005. Powered by a two stroke, twin cylinder, liquid cooled twin carburettor, dry sump engine.

Work on the 125 competition Motob?canes were started by Eric Offenstadt in 1974. In 1975 the factory created its own racing department which was entrusted to the engineer Jean Bidalot in 1976. Two riders Thierry Espi? & Michel Baloche participated in the French & World 125 cc speed championships of 1976.

This bike is now available in Switzerland and is delivered with some spares (list available upon serious request). A certificate of authenticity signed by Michel Baloche will be delivered with the bike.
