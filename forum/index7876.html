Title: Re: WTB: titled Kreidler??
Post by: Lindar on February 03, 2010, 01:03:20 am
We just take the MO bill of sale to the treasurer's office, hand it to them, pay them money, and walk out with a plate and registration papers. They mail you the title a few weeks later. The only snafu is that sometimes they decide that you need to get it inspected, which would be a problem if we didn't have the moped with us. If that happened though, there are two satellite offices where you might get a different answer on another day. I also have a thing I found on one of their websites that says that motorized bicycles do not need to be inspected. Whether that trumps the "out of state vehicles need to be inspected" part or not, I'm not sure, but I'm going to print it and bring it along next time I go there just in case.
