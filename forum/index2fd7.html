Repair and Performance / Re: Got my bike! ... Now what?
« on: September 22, 2010, 11:40:34 am »
Cheap and easy way to get the rust out:

Get a bunch of nuts and bolts and metal shit. BBs work too. Throw them in your tank. Tape up any holes where they could get out.

Put the tank in a small box and pack it with some paper or other packing material. Tape the box shut.

Put the box in your dryer (or do it at the laundromat). Pack all around it with blankets or sleeping bags or other soft stuff.

Put the dryer on cold air and run it for an hour or two.

Pull the tank out and rinse it out really good with water.

Done.
