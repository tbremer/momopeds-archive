I found out that I needed a generator for my Harley and they are neither cheap nor easy to find. I did find one reasonably priced in the Bikers Choice catalog, but since they only sell through retailers, I had to find one willing to not jack up the price to bad. The local dealership wanted $300 and my local parts supplier had previously tried to get me to pay $48 for $3 worth of parts, which I took as a personal insult. So I tried Motor Care in Lawrence, Tim the guy that runs the place was not only friendly and helpful, he quoted  me a price of $120. I was stoked and ordered it on the spot. He also sells some scooter stuff, I'll find out more when I go to pick up the parts.
