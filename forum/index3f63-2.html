I've found the problem with mirrors is that you need for them to go out far enough to see around yourself. and the longer the rod, the worse the  vibration  and it blurs everything. The bar end mirrors are nice , but they make the bike that much wider, and are the first thing to hit stuff. The rod and clamp type can be mounted higher, and inside, but they can sometimes interfere with brake and clutch lever stuff..... so each has its problems, but I can hardly ride without one.
