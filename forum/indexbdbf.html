The sad fact is when people are asked the same question for years they tend to be short and sound pissed, im trying to never get to that point.

Buy some smaller knarps here : https://www.treatland.tv/moped-mini-knarp-cable-stop-p/moped-knarp-mini-cable-stop.htm
and then you can use brake or derailer housing from a local bike shop (firs tsee how much they charge a foot cause sometimes it gets expensive. if it is use your old housing and cut it down) also buy a derailer cable for a road bike to use as a throttle cable. i also use tri-flow (teflon based repellant lubricant, also found at bike shops) to make sure everything slides free. keep in mind in all instances bike cables WILL stretch a little so leave some room in your adjusters to make up for it when it happens or pre stretch the cable.
