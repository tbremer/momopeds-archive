Title: Re: 103 Crank / Clutch bell Noises -- Video.
Post by: R.I.P. on May 13, 2011, 04:18:09 pm
i have had some experience with this. when i torqued the bolt on the variator it kinda binded with the crank and wasn't spinning freely. so what i did was took it off and looked at where the inside bearing touches the crank and clutch (in my case on the hobbit motor) you could see an indention where whatever was behind it was rubbing too high on the variator bearing causing it to lock up.

i took a thin washer that was the same size as the o.d. of the bearing race and drilled and dremeled it out to fit on the crank as a spacer to keep the 2 parts separated a fuzz more. i have had to put it on all of my motors and it works good. i'm able to torque it super tight without the bind shit happening.
