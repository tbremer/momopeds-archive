I'm gonna guess that your jet is too big. I don't have any Batavus-specific info, but mopedjunkyard says stock jets are sizes 52ish to 58ish. The drilling jets page in the wiki puts that at Dellorto size 50 and under. Now, I don't know that those Bing equivalencies are correct, but I'd guess that you should be under 60 at least. Also, if you downjet, that'll help you when you're running, and if you put on an air filter, that should help you get it started (and probably make it run better, assuming the jetting is right).
