I know nothing about Vespas so I can't tell you whether it's worth trying to salvage that ped or not. However, if you do decide you want to bring it back to life, you should be able to get parts pretty easily. For stuff like major engine components, the buy/sell forum at mopedarmy.com is probably your best bet. I've heard that Zippy Mopeds is a good supplier for Vespa parts, but places like Treats and 1977 mopeds should also have stuff you can use. There's a Vespa manual at the MRA if you need it, although you've probably got your best resource in the people here. There's nothing that can cure a broken moped quite as well as a dedicated fix-it day and a dedicated bunch of people to help you figure out what's wrong.

Shop links (treats, 1977, Zippy, and many others):
//www.mopedarmy.com/wiki/Shop_links

Manuals:
