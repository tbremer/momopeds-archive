I just saw a spitfire conv. or gt (can't remember which) for sale at the all british car show.  Look for the St.Louis Triump Owners Association classifieds (it is abreviated as SLOATA I think).  I like the Spitfire Gt's alot, they look like a mini jag and have a small 6cyl.
The Gt's are also cheaper most of the time becasue folks want little convertable british sports car not a hard top.

I have been driving my MGB to work the past few mornings with no top, coat, ridig helmet and goggles.  I really wish I had a nice cozy MGB GT or Spitfire GT.
Some reasons to love the Gt's-Convertable tops are expensive, plus install can cost, there is road noise, a little rain always finds its way in, keep your doors unlocked or the top will get slashed so someone can steal loose change, the car probably looks like shit with the top up, hmm probably more things to add to the list. Oh, the structure of the car is much stiffer.
Some reasons to love the convertable-hmmm, ummmm, it is nice when it's not to hot and not too cold and not raining, and rocks don't fly up off the road.

In all honesty I think both are great fun but perfer the Gt's
