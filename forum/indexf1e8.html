Haven't posted on here in a long time.
Mazzer sold me his Hobbit a few months back since I've been looking for one and decided to do a full out build on it. Clayton has been putting it together and modifying things to make it work.
The Ped: 1978 Honda Hobbit
Performance
Polini 70cc kit
"Race" crank and new bearings
Malossi clutch springs
Polini 21mm carb
MLM pipe
TJT variator
Vari ramp plate
HPI CDI
Malossi gears
Pretty sure I'm missing a lot as I'm just going off memory.

Cosmetics
Grimeca Mags
Low bars
280mm springs
LED tail light
Dual LED headlights
EBR forks
Stretched (not happening until winter)
Front disc brake (winter)

Again, probably missing stuff that will get filled in as time goes. Pictures to come and Clayton or Mazzer may add some also.

Thanks for checking it out!
