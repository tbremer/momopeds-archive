Title: Re: Grinding of piston rings?
Post by: worthlessckfsht033 on June 29, 2009, 11:59:02 pm
go here and read this, see if it tells you the spec's you need for your piston.
//www.mopedriders.org/article_view.php?faq=2&fldAuto=43

then try to get the right rings.

i bet rings from a similar piston would work fine, I've successfully used Husqvarna chainsaw rings in moped pistons.

just keep in mind that certain cylinder linings are only compatible with certain types of rings.

I think that jug is cast iron, so ring's intended for a cast jug would be best.

so that's a yes on grinding (dremeling would be best), piston rings are hardened and brittle so be careful.
