I run Opti 2 or Amsoil Saber interchangeably at 100 :1 all the time in all my bikes. It is very important that you have no air leaks. It is really the best in the winter the cold air kinda compensates for the extra heat that can be generated. However I have had no problems with seizing even in summer once I paid very close attention to the no air leaks rule. The other thing I have noticed is that when doing plug chops the plug colors will be different than with normal 2 stroke oil.
