General Discussion / The Magnum ate some of Mike's finger.
« on: August 05, 2009, 12:36:49 pm »
First off, he's okay!  But yeah, the Magnum ate a pretty big chunk out of his middle finger this morning when he was getting ready to go to work.  He started it, went to fix the back brake, and his finger got caught between the chain and the gear for a second.   There's some empty space behind his fingernail now.  It looks pretty gross!

Anyway, we've been at the hospital all morning, and he's in surgery now.  At first the doctors thought they'd have to amputate it at the top joint, but after looking at the x-ray, he's got enough bone left in there to keep what's left!  So they're doing some sort of cross skin graft--basically borrowing skin from the finger next to it, and replacing that finger's skin with some from his arm.
