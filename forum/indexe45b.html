The problem I had was that I couldn't get the gap close enough.  The points were riding the lobe just fine but it was still to big of a gap.  My mechanic took a second screw driver and placed it in the little slit on the points and closed the gap to the correct gap.  Sparks fine!

I got one ring free last night with some Sea Foam, the second is going to need some soaking.

That slit right above the point adjusting screw is were he put the second screw driver.  Mark, notice how much meat is on the part that rides on the lobe.
