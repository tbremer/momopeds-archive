Title: Bullit Moped
Post by: Holmes on November 26, 2009, 11:49:58 am
Here is my current winter time project.  It is actually two of the same bike being built two ways. I hope to have the Yellow one done within a month.  The blue one will be done with a few more hours of work.   I'm going to be lazy and refer you to my blog to catch up //www.holmeshobbies.com/blog/?page_id=20

The blue bike has a rear hub motor, that is about two-ish horsepower.  Top speed will be right at 37mph on flats and 42mph downhill. 

My yellow bike will have a three horsepower electric motor, the Astroflight 3210-6. The wheels are a mix of mountain bike and motorcycle parts. I hope to keep the final weight under 30 kilos, top speed around 38mph on the flats, and at least 20 miles of range at 30 mph. 

(//www.holmeshobbies.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2009/11/l_640_480_77FD64C9-A5EA-4E7B-89A8-CB4478DF4F40.jpeg)

Here is a comparison between mountain bike wheels and my hybrid wheels with 17" motorcycle rims

(//www.holmeshobbies.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2009/11/l_640_480_F0198B09-A715-4140-A5FE-C9CFC9963F86.jpeg)


The newest development is that I am getting a two speed gearbox made for me.  I am building a jig to make a new swingarm, and the reduction box will be housed internally.  This will cut down on noise a lot, and also protect the driveline.  1st gear will be 20mph and 2nd about 38mph. 

This morning I got the battery boxes welded up for the blue bike.  Attach these to the frame and it will be ready to run.
