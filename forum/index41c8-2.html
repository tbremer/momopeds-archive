My new project- a Kamasura VRX 250 BMX bike
« on: March 23, 2011, 06:20:51 pm »
Okay- last October, I acquired a "rare" Kamasura VRX 250 moped. (They called it a FINE ASIAN MOTORCYCLE...)








Now the bike is a typical POS- when I'd pedal fast enough for the engine to start to do a putt-pupuputting..the chain would come off the rear sprocket! And with a fully enclosed chain guard- it sucks!  So, I picked up a Dyno BMX bike on Craigslist for $25, and did a transplant!

Here it is on the Dyna, next to a DiBlasi R7E for comparison:





The big challenge is creating an extender from the crank to the pedal. The temptation is to just replace the really nice GT/Dyno crankset with a less substantial one.
After all, this is to provide human power, as well as to get the wee beastie started.
