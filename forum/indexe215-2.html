You should be in the mid 60's to the low 70's highest.  Otherwise there must be an airleak with a super mild kit like that.  People are running high 60s-mid 70s w/ cut cranks,drilled intakes, wild pipes, and wilder kits. 

I ran a 57cc dmp w/ stock crank, undrilled intake, boost port, dykes ring and it ran perfect w/ a 54 but the dmp is a strange undestructable kit that I actually really like-now in mark or eugenes possesion.  The olympia looks strikingly similar to the dmp.

mallossi, small circuit, drilled intake, case matched, 13.13 ran a low 73 or 74 but it was a bit rich.

polini non case matched, no intake drilling, polini country, 13.13 w/ a 68 very rich-my bothers.

not finished but a mazzi crank, 71cc pinasco, speed case, 17.5mm phbg seems to want something lower than mid 90's but really hard to tell untill I get a new cdi and can finish tuning.

Lots of times there are leaks at the base gaskets, the intake shim on the carb, and occasionally the head.
