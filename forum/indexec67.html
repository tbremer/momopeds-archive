Next issue: belts.  I used a random online calculator to determine what new belt to get. The result it gave was wrong. (Wrong info on the web! is that even possible?) So I figured it all out. Here's my formula:

BL= ((D-d)/tan(theta)) + pi/2(D + d + (theta/90)*(D-d))

Where:
theta = sin^-1((D-d)/2C)
D = Large pulley diameter
d = Small pulley diameter
C = distance between pulley centers

V belts are sold in inches so maybe do yr measurements in inches.
The length printed on a belt is it's outside length.
Think about this when making your measurements

My bat had a 3L310 on it.  The fist three means it's 3/8" wide on the outside. the L310 means it is 31.0" long. a 4LXXX belt is 1/2" wide (4/8"). Neat.
