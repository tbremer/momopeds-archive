I had an awesome time, even if some of it was spent in the rain working on cars, not mopeds.

Everyone seemed chill and I met a lot of cool people, saw cool bikes, rode a city I've never been to before.... however if the city i lived in had a street structure like Louisville, I'd have to quit mopeds and cars all together. There are ups and downs of any rally..as long as you come back in one piece, and hopefully so does your ped... its a winning rally.
