that appears to be the 505 motor,, the pedals go thru the motor,,, that motor is shared by a number of bikes,,most commonly the columbia commuter,,, the 505 shares a number of interchangeable parts with the 504,(of the foxi line), ie head, cylinder, piston,clutch  and carb/intake arrangement,, with the 505, usually an easy fix can be made to make the rear wheel a coaster brake,,, as to back  pedal to brake,,, a nice . feature.
  speed modifications can be made ,, but clutch problems really raise their ugly head, once, 70cc is exceeded
