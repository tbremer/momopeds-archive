lets see here... Sundays ride fail chain of events...
Bolt holding rear break plate from moving breaks (new style five stars have a shorter plate) allowing the plate to spin with the wheel. Thus pulling on the cable until it breaks. This quick jolt to the rear wheel then slaps the motor right in the face, causing the flywheels momentum to shear the Woodruff key. With the motor now out of time, it may or may not have misfired causing a soft seize.... 
After replacing the Woodruff key and fixing the break plate issue, before pulling the jug because the motor still spins freely without any drag or catch, I drill start it. Surprise surprise, it starts..... Until it warms up to right on 200?..... Let it cool down.... Repeat 3 more times... Still bogs out and dies right when my temp gauge hits 200?...so now I pull the jug and find the seize... Boo me....
