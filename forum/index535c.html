I would guess that using a bulb rated over the light coil's output will slowly kill the coil.  By running a low resistance bulb you will be pulling so many amps through the coil that the voltage drop is enough that it can't kill the light.   Has anybody running 55w bulbs noticed the coils turning brown?

I bet a wall wart could be used to bump down the voltage to a respectable but unreliable level.  I am going to do some experiments on my Honda and see if there is a better way.
