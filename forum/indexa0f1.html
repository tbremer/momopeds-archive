The handlebar issue: You just disconnect all wires, pull them through, no need to cut....a little dish soap help lube this . You may want to keep the stock electrics, cause they are great, its tricky to get the lighting right with aftermarket shit (headlight, taillight, brake light)

The revving issue... On  those Habbits, sometimes the stock carb throttle arm can get stuck open. The cable may be too tight or something blocking it. Its a rats nest up in there, but it may be something to look at.
