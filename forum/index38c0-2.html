Anyway, as things go, I'm relatively okay. They finally took off the evil neck brace, which I took as a sign that I was  FINALLY allowed to tilt my bed up and make my back stop hurting. I'd be better if I had a pillow and something real to drink (not whatever I can suck off their friggin spongesicle),  but I'll take what I can get.

As far as injuries, I am told that my ankle is pretty well annihilated. It's supposedly missing all kinds of skin, which they might have to harvest from somewhere else in order to put me back together. I think my ankle is also fractured, but not like "bent in half with bone sticking out" fractured. It has a "wound vac" on it right now ... sounds fun, right?
The only part of me I can really get a good look at is my arms, and they're a rather lovely reddish-blueish purple color. I'm hoping they turn green and yellow as they heal so I can say they were all the colors of the rainbow. I also got a decent amount of road rash, which I'm kicking myself for because I HAD my riding jacket ... strapped to the back of the moped. I guess my next purchases will be a summer-weight riding jacket that I'll actually wear and a good pair of motorcycle boots. Seriously though guys... wear your helmets and gloves. The only parts on me right now that are not bruised, broken, or scraped are where my gloves and helmet were. As for the broken pelvis, that doesn't hurt at all for right now at least. I suppose that'll hurt like a SOB once they make me get up and move around (although they're saying I won't be walking for a while, probably) but whatever. It could have been a lot worse.
