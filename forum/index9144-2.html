Title: Re: New Piston break-in?
Post by: Mike Pee on May 18, 2011, 07:21:33 am
There's all sorts of conflicting stuff about break in.  Some people say to go easy on it for the first 300 miles.  But yeah right, it's a moped.  It seems like everyone says the first 20-25 miles are the most important.  So maybe baby it a little for them, then go all out.

Then there are the people that say break it in the way you're gonna ride it.  And on a moped that means full blast all the time, so...  shit I duno.  Too much contradiction.

But make sure your rings are gapped right.  That's the main thing.  Then ride it?
