August 30 -- September 1
Please RSVP - Register Here (//docs.google.com/spreadsheet/viewform?formkey=dDdteTZ0dGM2ZnloaE9HczJpMlBBTlE6MA)

MMP is back for the fifth installment in our Rally series. This year, A two day round trip moped ride to Herman's Hole - //hermannshole.blogspot.com/ (//hermannshole.blogspot.com/) and back again. Leave lovely  CoMO Saturday and cruise the scenic missouri back roads to Herman's Hole, Herman, MO. Camp at the swanky|semi-famous skate park, then get up, clean up and ride back to CoMO on Sunday.


Friday
Registration begins @12:00 PM
716 West Sexton
Columbia, MO 65203
--
Pizza Tree Cruise Friday Night
--
Camping Spaces available for Friday in CoMO

There and back again is about 149 Miles. Gear and tune for country hills and thrills, pack speed is as always, 30-ish MPH.

Saturday
Ride leaves @ 11:00AM-ish
This is a destination w/ camping ride!
YOU WILL NEED A TENT!
Bring your Food/Drink & Drunk for the weekend.

Sunday
Ride back to CoMO leaves @12:00 PM
