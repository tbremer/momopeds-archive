From today's tribune...

Scooter-rider fights off would-be robbers

Friday, May 1, 2009

Police are investigating an attempted robbery after a 19-year-old man reported being confronted by two men while riding a motor scooter early today in north Columbia.

At 1:09 a.m., the victim was riding a motor scooter at Derby Ridge Road and Typhoon Court when the two men confronted him and one indicated he had a gun, police said. They demanded the victim?s scooter and other property and punched him.

The victim was able to fight off the men, who ran away. Nothing was stolen from the victim, who received minor injuries, police said.

The suspects are described as black males in their late teens or early 20s, about 6 feet tall and 160 pounds with clothing covering their faces. Anyone with information is asked to contact CrimeStoppers at 875-8477 or online at 875tips.com.
