I fixed two of these a couple weeks ago.  Both were Volvos, but I'd be happy to share what I learned about the process. 

A few important lessons:
1) compressor isn't always shot.  often it's the gap between the magnetic plate and the compressor that gets out of spec. 
2) you can't "over-charge" the system with a can of R134a
3) if it won't take coolant, you might have to jumper the low-pressure sensor on the coolant line.  You can fill a system with a can if the compressor isn't turning.
