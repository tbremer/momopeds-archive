Got the motor and gearbox mounted, and drivetrain mostly finished.

If this build doesn't rip I will either double the motor length (doubles torque and almost doubles peak and continuous power) or put a huge hub motor on it.   Should be good for an easy 40mph, but the battery range is always the kicker.  I may have enough batteries for about 20 miles at that speed, or 35 miles at 25mph.   Really makes me want a hybrid so I can get that stealth running around town with gas for long distance.   Or Maybe just two bikes that run at the same time...


I got some more work done on the electrics, encased the throttle control box (controller DSP) in epoxy for weather proofing and got the motor speed controller in.   The controller will tuck behind the seat post tube under the gussets. 

Need to make a few battery boxes and tidy up the wiring next.  Rear brakes would be nice too.
