Got the throttle working, using a new knarp making technique. Took a cable barrel end protector made a hole in the side with an awl. Put the cable through the hole and pushed it out the open end. Stuffed the barrel with fine copper wire then trimmed the excess cable, fraying and splaying it with a small screwdriver, then filled the barrel with molten solder using a soldering iron and rosin core solder.
