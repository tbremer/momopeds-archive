Title: Disc Golf Party in KC at Cliff Drive Aug. 20th
Post by: Optik on July 19, 2011, 08:12:58 am
The annual "Lions' Den" disc golf party is set for August 20th. This is not a tournament. It is a celebration of one of the best courses in KC (Cliff Drive course is in Kessler Park just east of downtown KCMO) and the people who have made disc golf great here. 3 bands, LOTS of good beer, BBQ, and all kinds of disc golf. This would be an excellent opportunity to play Cliff Drive if you haven't played it before. Also a great deal for an awesome meal, as much beer as you can swill, AND live music (only $10 if you pass on the shirt/disc shwag package).  Hopefully this won't conflict with any rally plans. Check out KCFDC.org for details.
