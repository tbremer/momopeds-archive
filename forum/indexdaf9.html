You can pull that brasstube that has the idle jet out with plyers, there is a 90 degree turn the fuel makes that easily becomes clogged and is near impossibly to change with out removing this pressed in brass tube.  The tube can be reset using a little epoxy or something to hold it in place.  If you clean the carb. without doing this it seems to always end up clogging again resulting in poor ideling and or not starting.  This is if it is similar to a Hobbit carb. wich in this respect I believe it is.
