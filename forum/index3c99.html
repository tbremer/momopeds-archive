New top speed, 54.8mph. I could probably get more with a bigger pipe and carb, but she just sips gas now so I won't be doing that.

Also found a side effect of the higher top speed. It starts to regen pretty aggressively after 50mph. Had a peak regen of 23 amps even though I wasn't telling the controller to do so. That pretty much settles it, I will be going to a freewheeling motor so I don't toast a freshly charged pack during a ride! I bet I can crack 60mph without the extra 1500 watts of regen on the top end...


I must say I didn't expect the DD hub forcing regen to be an issue, but I also didn't plan on kitting the bike. If course I could get a faster wound hub to lower the regen voltage, but if I am going to relace a wheel it won't just be a different wind.


This bike is pretty much my daily driver now, especially considering my wife is taking the car most days. Once I get all my business mail heading towards the shop I won't need a car for anything but staying out of crappy weather.
