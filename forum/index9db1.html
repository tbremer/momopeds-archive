« on: February 24, 2010, 07:14:25 pm »
Thanks guys for all you ideas for how to get a Puch kit on a m56.  I will eventually get that project started, but its going to have to be after I finish my original fast Bat project. 

I am running two m48s side by side.  The inside of the two cases are cut down by 1/4 inch and the cranks are also shortened and linked together with a lovejoy to reduce vibration and strain between the two engines.  They are going to be linked at 0 degrees, partially to reduce strain on the connection, and partly because I am only going to run one coil split between two heads.   I have ran into a snag here an there, but the main thing keeping this thing from going is time at this point.  However, I do have some questions, like should I run a single carb with a split intake or two independent carbs.   Anyone linked two engines together before, or have advice?
