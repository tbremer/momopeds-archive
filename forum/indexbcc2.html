From one of my underworld sources:
"Anyways I think you wanted to know how the setup on the puch turned out, so case matched za50 with 18x36 gearing, treats 70cc reed valve, parma reeds, daelim intake (bored out), techno boss exhaust and dellorto SHA 16.16. Anyways with my 210 lbs and 20 lbs of tools and fiberglass saddlebags I am still getting 51 on the GPS while seated, haven't tried a full tuck yet. I highly recommend that kit it seems good quality, the milling is real clean, lines up well with intakes and performance exhausts didn't really have to do a whole lot of port matching, just matched the engine cases."
