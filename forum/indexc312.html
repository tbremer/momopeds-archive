Hello,

 It was a very nice nite to ride last night and we had a pretty strong turnout, about 11 people showed up, including, dare I say, new members Glen and Becky?- (I'm horrible with names but I think thats right). We circled the park track multiple times, some lollygagin, some all out...we goofed on on the bike trails a bit, and grouped up to shoot the shit here and there. The weather was optimal for moped performance -cool air and clear nite. No one broke down, though we did get seperated (miscommunication), but we finally all met up for Ted Drews finest treats on Chippewa.

  So this ride was a definate success, it is one we'll yearn for when the winter sets in!
