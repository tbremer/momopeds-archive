Title: Honda Cub rebuild
Post by: Holmes on February 13, 2012, 10:54:49 pm
Jared was so kind as to give away an old Cub 50 (or super cub, or something 50) that he bought years back and didn't fix up.  Alls I wanted was a frame to start with, and so RK and I stripped the bike down and had our way with it!   I seem to have a lot of parts for the honda 50s from back in my days of pit bikes, so I started collecting them together in anticipation of the build.  For the most part it will be a bolt and build bike!

The plans for the bike will be daily driver and back up bike at rallies.   I will probably have a wheelset with dual sports for going to the farm, I will be lacing my own wheels anyway so why not make an extra set?
