The computer that I had all my programs (AutoCAD mainly) on for this stuff needs a new battery charger.  Besides that it isn't too hard.

When measuring them though you need the circumference of each cone section and the length of the cone, but not measured along the surface.  If you measure along the surface it will change the distance based upon the angle of the cone.  Or you can do the surface, it just means I have to calculate the actual distance between the two circumferences. This is what led to the really weird numbers in one of those PDF files I posted.

I can do circuit pipes too.
