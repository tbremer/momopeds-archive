Ride with a Broken Foot?
« on: April 23, 2010, 08:27:18 pm »
In my excitement to attach wheels to my Grande Project, I carried parts bare-footed into my Basement shop.  I snagged my left pinky toe on a cardboard box of volvo parts and snapped it like a twig. 

After x-rays, a visit to a podiatrist to get the toe "re-aligned," and the acquisition of a Nike-pump/Darth Vader boot, I can barely walk, stand, or straddle a moped.  No way in hell can I run or pedal-start my bike. 

You guys seem to severely injure yourselves often.  Have any of you broken your toes?  If so, how long did it take before you could ride your 'ped again??  Seems very unlikely I'll be takin' part in this year's rally...

'Course I feel like a wimp seeing how bad Matt got banged up.  The pinky toe is what I'd vote to amputate if I had to pick something, but damn... if that little buggers sticking out funny, you can't walk or stand properly.  Yet you are pretty much "intact" in every other way.
