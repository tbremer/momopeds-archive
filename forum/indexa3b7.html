TOWER PUB @ 3234 Morganford Rd

They have trivia on Thursdays. And it's not hard shit like @ Lemmon's. Not that Lemmon's is bad...but the trivia there makes me feel dumb.

http://maps.google.com/maps?sourceid=ie7&rls=com.microsoft:en-US&ie=UTF-8&oe=utf8&um=1&q=tower+pub+st.+louis&fb=1&view=text&latlng=662381163105821788

A review:
"Where typically gloriously unpretentious South City corner dive meets "hey, it's OK if you don't want to drink shitty beer" neighborhood rejuvenation.  It's the type of place that doesn't necessarily do anything spectacularly, but it does just about everything well.

The bar is fully loaded, and you can get your bucket of PBR longnecks along with your 3 different styles of Schlafly on draught and various fancier brews.  Pool table?  Check.  Jukebox?  It's there behind the tiny area where there may or may not be a bar band.  There's a weekly trivia night, TVs where they'll put the Cards game on for you if you ask, good drink specials, and an excellently restored bar that practically begs you to belly up for a couple.

The bartenders are talkative and friendly, and the place is full of typical neighborhood folks without devolving into that asphyxiating, insular haze that befalls so many south STL bars.  Just a great place to grab a drink or two, with seemingly no scene attached."
