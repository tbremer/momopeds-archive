Hardwiring is fun and easy, I promise. You don't actually need to know what you're doing. Just find all the pieces that you don't want or need, remove them and the wires going to them, and then hook up wires to what's left. It's a little more complicated if you want to keep stuff like the horn and brake lights, but if you're just removing wires from the existing wiring setup, then even those aren't a big deal.

http://www.mopedarmy.com/wiki/Puch_wiring
