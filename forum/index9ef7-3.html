MARDI GRAS - 2/13
« on: February 01, 2010, 12:52:33 pm »
If you are reading this, you are invited.  PM me for address if you don't know it, it's not far from the shop.  MidMo, BM, CTs GET HERE.

So we need to start planning.  This is a complicated event.  You show up, and get fucking wasted.  Now, I know it sounds simple, but it's not.

This year we STRONGLY encourage the most ridiculous costume you can find.  Anything will work, it really doesn't matter what it is.  More absurd = better.

The basic plan is:
- We leave our place at 11am to venture into the fray.  By this time you will have been at our place for an hour or so, enjoying a Full Irish Breakfast (car bombs).
- Go to the parade, fight for beads, enjoy the chaos, see boobies, step in vomit, fight in lines for portapotties, etc.
- Head back to our place when bored.
- Drink Angry Piranhas
- No one will remember anything after this, or it will at least be real hazy, so I'm not gonna plan any further.  Just go with the flow.

TIPS:
- Bring booze to share.  All kinds are welcome.  We need lots of vodka, limes, and sugar for Angry Piranhas.  Bring those especially.
- This is a marathon drinking event, not a race.  You need to set your own pace and plan to be drunk for 10+ hours.  You will notice extra hair on your chest on Sunday.
- The intensity with which you fight to catch beads is directly related to how much you've had to drink.  More booze = more desire for shitty Chinese plastic.
- There will be so many bros that it will blow your mind.  Brace yourself, bro.
- SMUGGLE BOOZE.  Not only are drinks overpriced, lines are super long and the drinks are all either shitty beer or vomit-inducing "hurricanes."  Flasks are your friend.
- Don't Y on our couch.
- Don't plan anything on Sunday.
