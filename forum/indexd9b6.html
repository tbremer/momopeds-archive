So I bit the bullet and got a spoke rolling machine.  This means that I can currently cut and thread any spoke in 15 through 13ga, and have custom dies being made for 12 and 11ga stuff.  I currently have access to plenty of spokes that work for mopeds (almost all 12 and 11ga), but now I will be able to make you anything you need.  Need some replacements?  Give the spoke diameter and length (in millimeters) and I will set you up.  Spokes length is measured by hanging them over a ruler, super easy.
