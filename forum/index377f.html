Repair and Performance / McGuyver Throttle Cable Knarp
« on: August 13, 2009, 09:12:51 pm »
I was installing a throttle cable and needed to install a knarp at the throttle. I've done a bunch of different methods including making a mold out of aluminum foil ,punching a hole through, put the cable thru, and fill with molten lead. This time I unscrewed the connector end off a spark plug drilled a hole thru it, trimmed it to fit, dremeled the threaded end off, and cut a slot in the end. Fed the cable thru the holes and screwed the threaded bit in, voila.
