Repair and Performance / Re: Pipe for my magnum
« on: April 04, 2009, 11:48:38 am »
you cant put a 70cc hi comp head on a 50cc cylinder.
it wont seal correctly.
you have to get the 50cc hi hi head.

if you dont want to kit out yet, your best carb option is getting a SHA carb, 15 or 16mm, and matching intake, 50cc hi comp head, and doing minimal port work. there are some pipes made for angled cylinders, they're just expensive.
