Re: electric problems?

behind the flywheel, is the stator, which has those copper wound things. Each of those is a induction coil, they produce electricity as the magnets in the flywheel pass. Seldom is there just one. only 2 is a rarity. Puchs have 4 , one is used to make the spark, involving the points, condensor, and High tension coil, the rest of the induction coils are divided up doin the horn, lights, etc.
   so its likely something has gotten miss wired, and your lights are robbing juice form the ignition.
   tools needed: wiring diagram, glasses, patience.
