1: Remove wheel
2: Get prybar or somthing like a screwdriver that is round, a box end wrnech works really well
3: Use tool to pry one side of the tire off of rim, once you get a bit off, use yer hands to pull the rest off. Be careful not to damage the rubber (I've torn them to bits and they were fine, butit's better not to). ONly take it half off. Now inspect for holes/damage
4: Remove old tube
5: Get some baby powder or jock itch powder or whatever you have on hand that isn't flour (prety much). dOUSE THE NEW TUBE AND INSIDE OF TIRE WITH IT AND COAT AROUND, GET RID OF EXCESS
6: Turn off capslock
7: insert new tube, making sure to remove nuts from the stem first. Leave te curved washer on though.
8:INsert stem and screw on the nut.
9: Make sure tube isn't crinked/kinked/pinched. Basically just straight and flat
10: USing your hands, start pushing the tire back on around the rim. USe your weight/muscles/teekenesis/or Yell at it in German to scare it back on. It may be difficult, but you CAN do it.

11: Re-check to make sure tube isn't wonky or twisted.
12: Fill qwith air, replace wheel and rock it the fuck out
