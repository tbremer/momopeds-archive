I found out that I needed a generator for my Harley and they are neither cheap nor easy to find. I did find one reasonably priced in the Bikers Choice catalog, but since they only sell through retailers, I had to find one willing to not jack up the price to bad. He also sells some scooter stuff, I'll find out more when I go to pick up the parts.
