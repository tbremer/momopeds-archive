Title: Re: Across the bridge?
Post by: Bangers on January 13, 2011, 12:54:53 pm
I live 45 miles from the K.C. limits to the south. As a practical matter I rarely ride to K.C. due to the time factor. I do however ride on the rural highways, mostly on the side of the road to get from town to town. It is legal as long as you keep off the Interstate, as a matter of courtesy and safety I try to stay off of roads without wide paved sides. When I go to K.C. for rides I load my bike into whatever vehicle I have and go. Saves time and worry.
