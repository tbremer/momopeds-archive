I wrote a paper about this once. I'd post it, but it's in Spanish... probably very bad Spanish.

Anyway, I remember my conclusion being that although two strokes are certainly not good for human health (namely because of hydrocarbons from the unburnt fuel and particulate matter from the burned oil), they aren't as bad for the environment (like, climate change/carbon dioxide, etc). Just tune your shit, use good oil, and try to comfort yourself with the fact that either (a) you ride your moped for transportation all the time, in which case you're reducing your need for the resources/pollution that comes from making new cars, or (b) it's a hobby, and there are probably a lot of other hobbies out there that create more pollution than a few mopeds.
