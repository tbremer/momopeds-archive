Little hint for the noobz. Place the pedal arm pin in, in the direction that will need the most space filled, ie the forward turning part. If you put them upside down your arms wont be so tizight.

Not that this info helped anyone but I thought it was very applicable since most people have pedal arm problems.. or no pedals at all
