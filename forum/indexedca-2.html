State law:
"Any two-wheeled or three-wheeled device having an automatic transmission and a motor with a cylinder capacity of not more than fifty (50) cubic centimeters, which produces less than three (3) gross brake horsepower, and is capable of propelling the device at a maximum speed of not more than thirty (30) miles per hour on level ground."

You're not legal because it's a 4 speed manual shift, and I bet it goes over 30mph.  But the cops in the city tend not to care about that stuff.  I can hit almost 50mph on my magnum, and I've done it with a cop behind me and they just went on their merry way.  I wouldn't worry about it if I were you.  And if it's 49cc, the DMV is just going to tell you to go away when you try to register it, even though you're supposed to.
