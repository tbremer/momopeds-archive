Honda C70
« on: March 18, 2011, 02:20:37 am »
After riding Kenden's scooter, I got the motivation to get a Honda C70 running. I picked it up last winter to help a buddy out and have been avoiding its 4 stroke weirdness ever since. Went to Wally World to pick up the smallest battery they carry and quickly found that it's too big and 12 v. Not to be dissuaded, I used a car stereo amp wiring kit to relocate it to the milk crate. Having read Harry Pellow's "Murphy is my Co-Pilot" the change over to 12v. didn't concern me and thus far no ill effects. The torque from the engine is amazing and the riding experience is more like my Harley without all of the "end of the world" exhaust noise. Ducks are not intimidated by its approach and will suicidally cross its path resulting in some pretty fancy skid stopping.
