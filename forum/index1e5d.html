Local Discussion (IRE) / 1st Annual Mary Jo Maroney Memorial Ride: 10/10/10
« on: September 06, 2010, 10:01:14 am »
There was probably no other scooterist in town with more spunk than MJ. She was a long time member of the local scooter revival scene. MJ was always about the scooters above all else. She didn't care what you rode, but that you loved scooters. She passed away last year on October 11th from a blood clot. She was battling very bad cancer at the time. We all cared for her very much.

MJ came to a few of the IRE fix-its and loved the mopeds too. She always admired them, and would have owned one if she could. Those of IRE that have met her can attest to her awesomeness. It'll be really cool to see IRE there in support and memory of her.

We'll be riding on the anniversary weekend of her passing, on 10/10/10.
Meet at the lower Muny Parking Lot. 11:00am.
We'll follow a ride through St. Louis, ending at Venice Cafe for a Mountain Dew toast, remembrances, and food if you're hungry.
