The spring for that thing has to be in that nob that sticks out.  Think of the Derbi pulley with the guts on the outside rather than on the inside.  Rather than the spring pushing against the engine as in the Derbi,  this one pushes against that circlip side.  The other side of the spring pushes the variator cheek. That would be my best guess.

I'd contact Rebel Moby and see if he has anything to say.  I'm willing to bet he'd have some info for yah.
