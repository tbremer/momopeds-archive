But anyway, yay fresh meat ... I mean, new people! I'm sure the IRE folks will help you out with whatever repairs or modifications you need/want to make, and I'm glad to see you signed up for the rally.

Usually the rally schedule goes something like:
Friday night: Hang out. Drink. Fix things. Ride. Realize you're hungry at 12:15, ride around getting lost a lot looking for somewhere that's still open. (I guess you won't get lost if you're a local...)

Saturday morning: Wake up. Find food. Be hungover or not, depending on how much you drank last night. Everyone congregates and fixes things that are broken. Announce that we will be leaving on the ride in 1 hour. Leave 2 hours later.

Saturday night: Like Friday night, but sometimes with more partying.

Sunday morning: Like Saturday morning, but sometimes with more hungoverness. Usually there's a smaller ride on Sunday for the people who can stick around for it. Then we all pack up and go home, feeling sad that we have to go back to the real world where everyone looks at us funny when we talk about mopeds.
