Well, Tom knows my secrets, but I'll post em for other peeps.  Start smoking roll yer own.  Makes smoking slightly more of a chore, and they're not as satisfying.  I did that for a bit, siked myself up to quit, then did it cold turkey.  If I got a craving, I kept a little bottle of lobelia extract around.  Dropper of that in some water takes the edge off, keeps me from being an angry prick for no reason.  After 3-4 days the physical shit seems to go away, then it's just breaking habits.  The first month I found it hard to be drinking with smokers, but after that it's no bother.

At this point I'll smoke a few when drinking.  Doesn't make me want to pick it up full time again.  The way I feel the next morning is a nice reminder of why I shouldn't do it.

Interesting effects of not smoking for a while: lot better sense of smell and taste.  Like, lots.  Also, you smell other smokers and are like...fuck, did I smell like that?  haha  You don't realize how filthy a habit it is till you're done with it.
