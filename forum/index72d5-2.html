I just got my first one a few months ago spent $500 and got robbed.  Take someone who knows what they are doing, or have them show you how to check stuff like the tank, compression, loose forks, clutch grab, spark, etc.  I'd avoid Ebay, that's where I got took, buying something based on the owners description, unreliable, and you pay up-front.  $500 should get you a nicely running E50, and I have to agree with Harry as a total Noob they (E50) aren't too hard to work on, super easy to find parts for.  I was thinking of doing a video documentary of my first experience, really wish I had now, got ripped, worked on it for awhile after a few frustrating months I asked for some help, met some cool dudes, found out the piston had been seized, got parts from the awesome Crazy Harry, replaced the top end.  Now it works, needs tuned.  Where are you from, if you're from CoMo I'll help you look, still looking myself, only I don't want any that run at all, managed to get two (those Puch's) in my Fusion this weekend.  So I know I can move E50's without a truck, just need to remove the front forks.
