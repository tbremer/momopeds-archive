Project Moby for sale. Before I part it out on MopedArmy, I figured I would give somebody here an opportunity to make this their very own.
Moby 50v:
stock ported? cylinder/s (exhaust threads stripped on both)
Leo Vince handmade pipe that has been reinforced and was used as a launch lever
Two Dellorto Sha 15.15 carbs
Sexy Long seat
Gazelle tires
EBR forks

I got this as part of a package deal but don't want to spend the time to reassemble this one. The kitty litter buckets are full of various extra parts and the box is just extra goodies that were left here. This bike was quick and really fun when it was running back in the day and you can have it for a pretty nice price. I don't know how much I want so make me an offer on what you could turn around and part out for a nice stack of cash. Let's start with $275 for everything.
