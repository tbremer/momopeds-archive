I went with a chrome Tecno Boss for Tomos A-35. It required minimal modification and looks oh so sweet all tucked in (no pedal or centerstand issues) and somewhat stock looking. I'm waiting on a 15 SHA and will be opening up the stock intake manifold as well as the intake and exhaust on the cylinder. The stock 11 tooth countershaft sprocket may be swapped for a 12, but I don't want to give up any more low end than necessary (it's a slug off the line with the stock setup). I'm not looking to go 50, just want to be able to keep up on group rides. 33 mph on the Grande didn't cut it this year.
