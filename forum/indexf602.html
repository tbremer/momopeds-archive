Title: Re: 2-Weel Mania
Post by: Lindar on August 12, 2011, 10:31:34 pm
On a Tomos, a bad clutch-side crank seal will cause you to burn copious amounts of transmission fluid, resulting in a cloud of white smoke. I would assume a Puch would be the same way, although it doesn't seem to be a common problem with Puchs. Or rather, I hear about bearings going bad a lot more than I hear about seals going bad on Puchs.

I did have a leaky flywheel-side seal on my Puch once, and the engine cases behind the stator plate were oily and black. It ran lean, but it didn't seem to affect the transmission fluid at all.

So maybe it depends on which seal is bad?
