So, after talking to Mike for a bit today I wanted to say that the secret of the CoMO ride wasn't supposed to slight anyone. I don't want anyone to be upset or anything that we did this on our own.

For me, I just wanted some time out on the road and como was an obvious choice with its proximity.

The only person that was supposed to know in columbia was Sam, because he was housing us, it was meant to be a surprise to both IRE and MidMO, in a way to see if it can be done, to see what it takes, and to just clear my head.

Please, don't think there were any ill-intentions in keeping this a secret. I, personally, thought it would be fun for you guys aswell to check the forum and just be like "oh shit! They're going for it."
