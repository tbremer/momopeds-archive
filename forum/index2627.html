Repair and Performance / Re: Sachs
« on: August 18, 2013, 01:02:01 am »
take a dremal or some other tool of metal destruction and cut/grind the area where the two ends meet on the clamp so that you can clamp it down into a tighter circle, don't be afraid to give the header a few "love taps" with a rubber mallet or something to make sure it is fully seated as well, at the baffle clamp, clean every thing up all nice like , no grease or oil goo, and slather on a little jb weld and then clamp it and bolt it back up and make sure the pipe is all lined up right  wait a few hours for it to harden up and go blast, well sachs blast...
