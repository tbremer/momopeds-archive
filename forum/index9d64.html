I finally made it over there just today.  Turns out it actually does belong to the guy who runs this little store.  He was there and I got a chance to talk to him and look it over.  He has his heart set on $300 because somehow that ebay listing (//cgi.ebay.com/1978-peugeot-103-Sp-Moped-Scooter-Complete-Must-See-BIN-/170518432722?cmd=ViewItem&pt=LH_DefaultDomain_0&hash=item27b3b0cbd2) makes him actually believe its worth $900.  The bike does look good.  No rust to speak of, nothing obviously broken, missing or out of place.  Probably has original tires that hold air.  Even has some tools in the little compartment below the seat.  He knows absolutely no history whatsoever.  Sounded like he just recently purchased it himself.  He says it does not run, doesn't know why or even if it ran when they stopped riding it.  Has an Oklahoma tag with a 89 sticker.  He assumes it hasn't ran since then.  I optimistically told myself to buy it and clean the carb and it would run, then my little amount of common sense reminded me that I know better than that.  I'm not up to speed on moped maintenance yet and I could very quickly find myself over my head.  I'm going to have to think about this one.  My female doesn't much care for the idea of this at the house, so she will probably avail.  Either way, I have not made up my mind.
