With it stuck at one speed you should be able to look and see that one pad is engaging and the other not.  You will either see a solid ring or two solid rings as they fly by.


So I envision this.  Put the clutch on, then slowly spin it up until you see one pad pop out... take it off, adjust screws, then try again untill you only see one solid ring of motion.


Right now I don't really care at what speed they engage, just that they engage together.  This will keep them from ripping apart.  Also cut down on vibration/chatter from being unevenly engaged.
