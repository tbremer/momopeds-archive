kerry*s patch.  where*s that?
i am kinda lost in north st louis although i love the area around florissant st. and where
that tower is.

i grew up on the south side.  right outside carondelet park.  that is my old neighborhood.
bellerive park was closest.  messed around all over south broadway and east to the river.
never went farther south than river des peres.  never farther north than virginia st.
back then it was safe to roam with no worries.  when i was in high school they were just
starting to rehab in soulard.  some high school friends actually had a couple shells in
soulard that they redid.  could buy a shell for 5,000 back then.  but everyone thought
you were a nut.  some didn*t go to college and just stayed and did rehabs for years.
now they own a couple rentals and are set for life..

college buddy of mine grew up in CWE.  his family bought a 3 story off pershing .  house was
a embassy house during the worlds fair.  bought it for 45,000 in 1970.  sold it for 450.000 in
1999.  neighborhood was bad then.  kept on getting wailed on by the people up north.
he used to run home after school from cathedral parish let out lest he get wailed on again.
now he is 6* 4**  and is kinda prejudiced.  wonder why.

we used to sneak into blues games at the arena on steamers tickets too.  great fun for cheap.
when i go back to visit st louis in the winter i always brings my skates, gloves and sticks to
play night hockey at steinberg rink after hours.  the guys there have been doing it for over
20 years.  says they give a 12 pack to the night watchman and its ok.
safe too.  what idiot is in forest park at 11pm on a winter night.
thats what i love about st louis city.
