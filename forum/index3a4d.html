"Cheers to the Troops!" Rollergirl USO Benefit
« on: July 22, 2011, 04:53:25 pm »


Here is the message from my gf Brittany (all messages regarding discounted tickets can be sent to britkamo@gmail.com):

I am part of the Arch Rival Roller Girls that is hosting a fundraiser on Saturday, July 30th and I would like to personally invite you to attend. There will be live music, magic, comedy & burlesque (after 10). A dinner buffet (6 ? 8 ) will also be included in the price of the ticket. The profits will go to the USO of Missouri.

Along with the food and entertainment, there will be a 50/50 raffle and a silent auction. The items available for auction are certainly worth the price of admission alone!

My fellow jeerleaders & I have been working hard on this for months and we'd love for you to join us, it's sure to be a fun evening.

Here are the details:

Cheers to the Troops ~ An Evening of Entertainment in Historic Soulard
Saturday, July 30th 6 p.m. - midnight (dinner from 6 - 8 )
South Broadway Athletic Club - 2301 7th, Saint Louis
Tickets are:
$15 for adults
$10 for veterans
$5 for kids 10 & under
FREE for Active Military!

Tickets will be available at the door or can be purchased online via Brown Paper Tickets ~ //www.brownpapertickets.com/event/180310

For you facebook savvy folks here is the link to the event ~ //www.facebook.com/event.php?eid=113828348704679

I want to be sure to mention that Maplewood Scooter Co., Anheuser Busch, and TRX Tattoos and Piercing among some of our other lovely sponsors have donated some very awesome items for our silent auction. If you guys want to get a group together and reserve a table ahead of time, I can offer a discount on the tickets. A table seats 8 and it would be $12.50 per person. Please let me know if you are interested!

Hope to see you there and please get the word out!
