The rest of the setup, as well as port alterations and such:

Exhaust raised (toward the head) ~3mm, with 1mm raised "booster" section 1/3 of port width. It's widened 1mm on each side, so 2mm total, and made square. Port matched to the 28mm Boss header.
Intake raised as much as it can be without bleeding into the piston boost window, lowered (toward the case) 1mm; port matched to my round intake pipe.
Transfers are as big as I could get, no change to the port timing or port size in the cylinder. Case matched.

19mm Clone PHBG w/matching intake
Boss w/red bull can on the silencer
16x42
3-shoe w/blue springs, 2 turns in from flush

I got a 6p Homoet the other day that gave me at least 2 or 3 mph on the top end, compared to the Boss. It's a crazy go nuts pipe. BUT, it doesn't hit 'till 35 or 40 and I have no power 'till then. Probably because I ported the cylinder to match the Boss, but whatev. Can anyone halp me get my low end back? I don't need to gear down. It's got so much power on top that I could probably gear UP a smidge and it'd still pull it.

I've got plans for that pipe if it doesn't work out with the KStar, though. It's all in the works.
