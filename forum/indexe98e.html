factory 2 stroke crankshafts are almost never balanced perfectly.
there is room for improvement

stuffing a crankshaft increases the case compression ratio .
case compression ratio is 1/2 of  cylinder compression ratio.
stuffing your crankshaft increases case compression ratio and pushes more mixture up to the cylinder= more power
