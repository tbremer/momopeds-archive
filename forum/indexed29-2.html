yo, my bike was that color. i got it from autozone. make sure when you do your bolt, or whatever your gonna do, to flash the paint. it'll save alot of detail time and look sharp.
cover the sides in blue tape
tape a piece of paper or two big enough to carry the bolt layout
look at that pic you did on ms paint
draw it out on the paper roughly, to size it up and get your points and the look you want
keep the tank on the bike til you get this right
look at what you got and clean up the lines
it's all good except where you fucked that spot up, fix it
measure around the tank to put it in the same spot on both sides
that's crooked, fix it
tape it in place and trace it and cut it out witha razor, not too deep
 if when your done, it looks like hell, get a roll of single pinstripe and line the edge
