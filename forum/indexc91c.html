what do your cylinders look like.?

is it the normal  2 transfers, 1 exhaust, 1 intake.

i have a Jawa 207  with slanty, cut in transfers.
sort of like the Puch cylinders with the open transfers up the cylinder walls.

Jawa 210 engines also have a weird transfers set up

seems these cylinders were not very common.

been talking to trevor on MA  and he is getting 43mph out of a Jawa 207 with the slanty
transfers, 18mm PGHG? carb and a hacked on pipe.

been into 2   Jawa 207 engines now and the engines are solid, well built.   tanks
about as easy to split as a vespa engine.
only special tool i needed was a universal case splitter......Tusk casesplitter.  on ebay .  50.00
crank bearings are 6203
gaskets are easy
seals were  easy
VLADO  helps

haven*t done  a Jawa 210 yet.
