I was comparing my builds the other day.

Metrakit 65cc:
$130 kit
$60 pipe
$90 carb
Total: $280 to go 52mph

Gilardoni 74cc:
$200 kit
$170 pipe
$120 carb
Total: $490 to go 58-59mph

Price makes it not worth it.  Increased maintenance on the big kits makes it not worth it.  I'll probably never build one of the big boys again.

Touching almost 60mph is real fun and terrifying though.
