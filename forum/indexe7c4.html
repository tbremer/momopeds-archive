http://www.slpl.lib.mo.us/cco/ords/data/ord6692.htm

Read it.  It covers a lot of stuff to do with mopeds.  Orange flags, how to ride, what you can carry, etc.

Prettymuch they made a law to make anything we do wrong.  haha


But mainly:
"        A. No person shall operate or ride upon a motorcycle or motorized bicycle unless such person is wearing protective headgear which complies with standards established by the Missouri Director of Revenue.

        B. No person shall operate or ride upon a motorized bicycle unless such motorized bicycle is equipped with a red or orange flag measuring not less than twelve inches by twelve inches. Such flag shall extend not less than forty-eight inches in height above that portion of the seat occupied by the operator.

        C. This section shall not apply to persons riding within an enclosed cab or on a golf cart."
