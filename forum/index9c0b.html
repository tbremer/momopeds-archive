My suggestions:
-Ditch the head gasket.  You don't need one, and it  will raise compression if you leave it out.  All my Puchs run without a head gasket except my kitted magnum.
-Forget the speedo.  They're inaccurate, plus they're innaccurate and annoying to keep working.  I bought a used GPS with a handlebar mount on ebay for $60, which is way more accurate and won't break unless it gets run over or something.
-Petcock for $15: //cgi.ebay.com/ebaymotors/puch-maxi-moped-petcock-fuel-valve-vespa-ciao-too_W0QQcmdZViewItemQQ_trksidZp1713Q2em153Q2el1262QQcategoryZ35620QQihZ008QQitemZ180275336350QQtcZphoto
-Bing bowl gasket for $3: //cgi.ebay.com/ebaymotors/bing-carburetor-float-bowl-gasket-puch-moped-maxi-carb_W0QQcmdZViewItemQQ_trksidZp1713Q2em153Q2el1262QQcategoryZ35620QQihZ008QQitemZ180274342127QQtcZphoto

While you're ordering from Treats HQ, take a peek at their Puch performance stuff.  I'm in love with this performance pipe, and it's only $60: //cgi.ebay.com/ebaymotors/puch-moped-BOSS-performance-pipe-CHROME-maxi-magnum-RAD_W0QQcmdZViewItemQQ_trksidZp1713Q2em153Q2el1262QQcategoryZ35619QQihZ008QQitemZ180276203578QQtcZphoto  Get that and a couple jets for your bing, and you should be doing 35ish mph.

Oh, and for the rust in the tank, I know a few people who swear by either Naval Jelly rust remover, or...some other thing Malaguti Mike claims is perfect...can't remember though.  But some people say the vinegar plan works great.  It probably depends on how bad your tank is.  If you want to go whole hog to strip and coat it, I used POR 15 kits on two of my Puchs, and they work great.  //www.por15.com/prodinfo.asp?grp=CTRK&dept=12  It's $40, but you can get two tanks worth of coating.  Maybe 3 if you were real careful.
