Title: Re: 2 stroke oil.
Post by: Valimagdon on May 05, 2009, 07:20:55 am
Quote from: Barney on May 05, 2009, 07:11:07 am
why no on the gallons       i know some one that uses it exclusivly with no ill effects. i said the same thing to him and he claims its the same chemical breakdown dif mark/pack

I call shenanigans on your friend. Every 2T oil brand and blend is a different "chemical breakdown." Every 2T oil is not created equal. Gallon jugs of outboard oil is fine if you are running stock and don't mind carbon deposit buildup in your engine. As you add on performance parts, or want your ped to run better for longer, youshould go with a semi-syn or full-syn oil. Regular ol' 2T oil just doesn't stand up to the stresses as well.
