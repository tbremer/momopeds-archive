Dmp unstuffed is a bad idea IMHO. You HAVE to drill or cut an oiling ring into the conrod before you go about doing anything, make sure there's no shavings anywhere in that sucker too. Honestly I'm a sucker for the Treats aluminum crank

Beez is pretty spot on with those jet.

The Polini is a beautifully manufactured kit that could see lots and lots of miles, but it needs to be babied and tunes properly!

Get your intake installed and make sure there is no warping or high spots in the piston. Make sure every port is chamfered and smooth an clean, make sure the intake is flat, you have to case match all the weird nooks and crannies from the base gasket.

Leter rip! Oh, I was running 16x45 with my 15 bing setup, it was torquey and revving pretty high.

Ask lots of questions!
