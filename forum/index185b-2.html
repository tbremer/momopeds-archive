Title: Re: Ride Aug 5th
Post by: Ryan N. on August 06, 2012, 10:55:59 pm
mini rotor: easier to set the timing on in my experience.  lighter (supposedly, I haven't had both at the same time to weigh them but they're both extremely light regardless).  bright lights, 50w+.  you can adjust the stator without taking the rotor off.

internal rotor: kind of a pain in the ass to set the timing (you line up TDC with a mark and kinda guess, use a timing light to get accurate).  super crappy light coil has a whopping 10watts of light power.  you have to take the rotor off to adjust the stator (meaning you also have to re-set the timing, ha!)
