First on the list of parts is a new rear shock.  Riding this POS is like being a fatty on a pogo stick.  The play in the rear suspension doesn't help inspire confidence while cornering either, so the pivot bearings need to get updated.   Another minor issue is my lack of rear brakes.  Guess I should get some that work. 


Perusing the world wide interwebs I came across a site that had a complete rear end with 12" wheel, disc brake, and rebound adjustable shock on it for $260 something shipped.  I was planning to spend that much on just a shock and some brakes, so I nabbed it up.  While I was there I got a 72cc kit too.  I figured since the rings need replacing I might as well replace the cylinder and piston at the same time.  At least the logic seemed good at the time...   Eh, $70 for a kit, I'm worth it. 

I also hate that the gastank is so fawking small and short, so I have this garelli tank here that would look pretty dope.
