I use wal-mart bicycle tubes, they're thicker but you have to add 3 inches to the size on diameter. So a 17 x 2-14 would be 20 x 2-14. They also carry heavy duty pre slimed and no-flat versions but these are too pricey for my budget currently. You should also look at your tires as they are your first line of defense, new is of course better than old, name brand is better than cheap, softer compounds will puncture more easily, and under inflation will compromise the puncture resistance of any tire.
