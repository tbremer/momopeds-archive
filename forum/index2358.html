I like the idea of a collective with our current gangs, obviously. I don't think we should rule out including other gangs, but I don't know how many more gangs in KS or MO might pop up. If we put Missouri in the name, then it might be weird for any other gangs that ever show up over here. So I like the more inclusive "No Coast" or "Mid Coast." Or we're all in UTM Zone 15N....  But yeah, then it does make it less inclusive. Do we really want gangs from like, Maine or Florida or Arizona though? The thing I really love about this site and all of you is that I know you and I've met all of you in person and I think you're all rad. The farther away other gangs are, the less likely we are to establish that kind of rapport with them, and I think we lose something in that. I don't want to make a Moped Army rival, and I think the best way to do that is to keep it regional.

And no unicorns. The Tom Cruisers already have a unicorn as their logo, and we don't want to make it look like we're stealing their idea.
