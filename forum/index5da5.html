Like mopeds? Like wrenching? Wanna make money at it?
« on: July 28, 2011, 05:38:26 pm »
For the last month, I've been working as an assistant mechanic over at Maplewood Scooter Company. Since I'll be starting my new teaching job next month, they're gonna need a replacement. COULD IT BE YOU!?!?

Your job will be mostly oil changes, tire changes, carb cleans, dealer preps, and other miscellaneous wrench work. The more you already know how to do, the better! You should know how to spin wrenches, be organized, and be comfortable with working on all sorts of bikes. Extra bonus points for electrical and general troubleshooting know-how.  It's mostly modern scooters there, but we do get the occasional 'ped. Mike Taylor, the head mechanic is a great guy, and fun to work with (as long as you put his tools back!).

They are located at Big Bend and Manchester Rd. You can go apply in person, and/or ask me any questions you may have.
