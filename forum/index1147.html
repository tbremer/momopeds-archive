My camera needs the special computer attachment thing. Hopefully I'll have them all up by tomorrow afternoon.

I had an awesome time, even if some of it was spent in the rain working on cars, not mopeds.

Everyone seemed chill and I met a lot of cool people, saw cool bikes, rode a city I've never been to before.... however if the city i lived in had a street structure like Louisville, I'd have to quit mopeds and cars all together.
