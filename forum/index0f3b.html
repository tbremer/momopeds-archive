alright, heres the sitch. I'm running a stock magnum cylinder and head, with a 14mm bing, 70 jet, and a proma gp pipe.

Simple enough, but I can't seem to make it run right.

After seizing the cylinder, crkt and I tore down the top end, and replaced it with a normal stock cylinder. Seized that one within 2 miles of his house. So after some head banging we split the cases and noticed that the small end bearing on the wrist pin seized as well. So we replaced the crank. All bearing are good, and so are the seals.

We put it all back together, and after a short test ride today, I seized the magdumb cylinder again! This time it was at the intake port. The first time it seized was on the tranfers. WTF!?

Took a look at the plug and there was carbon build up between the terminals. Time to downjet, but I don't think that downjetting will fix my problem...

Any ideas?
