your going to have to run a "waste spark" system, don't worry it's not that mysterious.

it's basically 1 coil with 2 output leads. the only thing is that the coil Ed mentioned has the igniter built in.

fear not, just buy the stator plate + flywheel, and no igniter

next get an old 80s Honda  2 stroke cdi unit.

then get a 2 wire ignition coil from something like a cl175 or any bike that ran waste spark ignition (usually 4 strokers)

install parts, wire ignition, and adjust the timing.

problem solved, now about that clutch. . .
