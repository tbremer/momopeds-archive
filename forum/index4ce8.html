The zoo is awesome but there is some construction going on (sea lion/aquatic fun stuff) right in the middle so it is kind of annoying and you can't walk straight to some places but it is still full of some cool animals. Grant's farm is pretty cool too if you wanna see/touch/play with some farm animals. As far as other cool places, the city museum is fun. It's not really a museum, it is more of a huge interactive art piece with climbing and slides and shit. I'm a fan of the Schlafly Tap Room and Schlafly Bottleworks too.
