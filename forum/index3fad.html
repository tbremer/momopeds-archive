PLEASE buy a helmet! It may just be your means of survival.
Please ride your stock moped in the lane of traffic, you are to be treated as if you are a motocycle not a bicycle.
Please learn and use hand signals, despite the fact that you have turn signals, that I don't remember seeing light up they are a helpful tool to others on the road.
Please stay off the big roads like Hampton (for which you turned onto off of Eichelberger). For your stock ped you are a  dangerous hazzard on the big road without having multiple other riders riding with you. and the fact that you were veering between the lane and the parking lane.

Now, that said I'm no expert rider, but  and I don't ride that often any longer but when I do, these are ALL things I choose to do for both my safety and the safety of others.
