Title: Re: Rigid Maxis?
Post by: Mike Pee on November 06, 2008, 10:29:49 am
When you make those rigid frames too fast they can break on that seam below the tank.  So a crossbar gets added to give strength.  Triangles are stronger than a U.

I've got a rigid maxi.  It's going to have a removable crossbar, not a welded in one.  I think.

Title: Re: Rigid Maxis?
Post by: Mike Pee on November 06, 2008, 01:20:45 pm
At 45mph on a normal maxi with suspension, you probably don't need to worry about the weld breaking.  It's the people doing 70mph that really need them.

A rigid maxi over stock speeds should probably have one.

And they sell removable ones.  All chromey and such.  Under $30 usually.
