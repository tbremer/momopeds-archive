Repair and Performance / has anyone?
« on: May 23, 2011, 07:57:26 pm »
tuned their clutch using the method described in moped army wiki, where it says to turn the springs all the way in, and then turn them out 720 degrees?  btw its for a e50.

Tips

Always use at least blue springs for your tuned clutch.
Never lighten the shoes under 55 grams.
Never turn the screws that hold the springs in too far. The springs need room to work.
**Max spring tension can be found this way: Turn the screw all the way in and then 720 degrees out. This is the max tension.**
Counter sinking the brace, so the brace bolts will clear inside the clutch bell like shown in the picture is pretty much necessary
