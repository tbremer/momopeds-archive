I was reduced to digging thru the trunk to find a cylinder to map, since I left the moped at home. Results: Justin's K-Star 50cc matched the port timing of Matt's Airsal 70cc with the exception of the exhaust port being 1mm lower at the bottom but the same at the top. The Airsal I believe was factory, the K-Star was port altered by Justin. The ports differed in shape with the Airsal ports being primarily square and Justin's being oval. As the dimensions were consistent, Justin's ports would flow less fuel/air. This not necessarily being a bad thing due to the 20cc aprox. difference in capacity. I then consulted the experts as to possibility of repairing the Airsal with the unanimous opinion being toss it. Being contrary by nature I will of course work on it to prove them all wrong.
