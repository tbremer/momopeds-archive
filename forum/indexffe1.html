Tomos top end on an E50
« on: April 15, 2013, 09:47:41 am »
I helped a guy kit his tomos with an a35.  As thanks he gave me his old top end, intake and carb.    Tonight I am going to start working on a Pinto, and I was wondering if an a35 top end would fit on an e50.  It would get me a larger stock intake, a better carb, and reeds, so the swap seems like a good idea. 

I remember from way back that Ryan K had put on on a Maxi N that he traded for his first derbi, but I don't know what all needed to be done to get it to work.

Drop you knowledge here

It is bolt on apart from the stud holes being m7... so either use it as is or make a bushing to take up the play.  The exhaust will need to be bent or rewelded.  If you use a tomos head you will want longer studs.
