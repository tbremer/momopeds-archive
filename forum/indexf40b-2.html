Check to see whether you have spark. If you have spark, it's probably a fuel problem. If you don't have spark, then that's probably the problem. Since it started after you cleaned the carb but not after you cleaned the tank, my bets are either water in the gas (sometimes it's reallllllly hard to get all the water out of the tank), or you got water in your electrical connections somewhere.

Fred's Guide is really helpful in situations like this too -- it helps you figure out what's wrong, and then it gives you ways to fix whatever you think is wrong.

http://www.mopedarmy.com/wiki/Fred%27s_Guide

How do you check the spark?
