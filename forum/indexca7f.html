Moby for cheaps!
« on: April 28, 2013, 04:13:36 pm »
I have a thoroughly project status Moby 50v that is taking up space in my garage. Put it in yours for the low low price of $100. It came to me with the engine in pieces in a Tidy Cat bucket and it will leave the same way. Needs a new crank, cylinder, bearings, seals, tires, tubes at a minimum and if you want to go all out a pipe, carb, and transmission setup. You might need to do that anyway and, at a minimum, invest plenty of time building up everything. The engine cases has the old bearings rusting way in them so some pb blaster and a week of soak time are in your future unless you go for a set of new cases. As far as I know, the engine bucket contains a complete everything, but I have never even taken it all out at the same time so who knows. 100 bucks and it will be your new friend.
