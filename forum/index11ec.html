O.k. Displacement. The pull start option was originally only on the 70/80cc model (which are both really 66cc the cylinder head chamber doesn't count) and required that the engine be located higher on the frame to clear the pedals or a wider crankshaft. Really its just information. I would follow the break in procedure since it sounds like it didn't get run much.
     Nothing gets added into the crankcase, its a real 2 stroke. It has a dry clutch.
