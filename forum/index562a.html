I just rebuilt some of those... I can't take a picture right now but here's the deal. The little tab's only purpose is to work the brake light switch. So the flat bit should be flush (ish) with the lever end. There should be a hole on the lever where the little nub on the tab goes. The long end of the spring goes down the handle and then you gotta wrestle with it until you find the sweet spot and get the bolt through. Help this helps but I know it prolly doesn't.... If you haven't reassembled it by tomorrow bug me and I'll take a photo.
