FIRST THINGS FIRST:
The time has come, and it's a big task. I am hoping for no more than 24 hours of downtime, but I can't predict the future.

My plan is two step:
   1. Move all the content
   2. Move all the databases

For the content, you all shouldn't see anything change. It might be a little slower since I will be migrating 7ish GB of data, but no interruptions in service.

For the databases, I am going to put the forum in maintenance mode to block all postings and save the DB structure, move it over to the new server, upload, and check for any lost data. And firing everything back up. -- This is the part I am more fearful of something going awry. However, I have 90% confidence (and some documentation) that tells me it will all go smoothly. I will change the Nameservers over on Saturday night (22nd) or Sunday morning (23rd) and hopefully have everything up and running no later than Monday morning (24th).

I promise to not lose anyone's data or change privacy / log in / sign up information.

SECOND ORDER OF BUSINESS:
I have said this before and I will say it again, please, please, please bring anything to my attention that you guys don't care for or like. If the colorway is shitty, let me know we'll figure something out. If there are weird colors or fonts overlaying each other or a white background with white text and you can't read anything, let me know.

If you need anything changed (permissions, users, member status, etc) and you or your gangs admin (Does every gang have an admin still?) cannot fix the issue, let me know.

I am just a text, phone call (though I rarely answer my phone), email or tweet (@_tbremer) away. I want to make this a place you love and I want it to be a place where more and more people are joining. LETS DO IT.

That's all! Thank you all so so much for donating!
