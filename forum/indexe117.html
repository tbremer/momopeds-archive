Banging on bearings is crazy.  Something that tiny, precision, and essential shouldn't be roughed up.

The only times I've messed up the oven/freezer trick was me being too impatient.  And the only ways I've done it is:
-got the bearing cocked sideways (always the damn clutch-side one).  This has never happened since I started coating it in 2 stroke on top of the heat/cold.  Oh, and started taking my time to line it up before pushing it on.
-forgot to put the oil seal behind the clutch side bearing.  Just complete retardation.  Only did that once.
