Your taillight and headlight are totally different. At least, they should be.
Ummmm, Your taillight shoul;d have one grey wire and one ground and blue-green wires from your brake levers to the block.

Does your brake light work? That would be a good place to start as well.

What did you change in the wiring block, original wiring pattern?

So, you KNOW you bulb is good, that's the first most basic step. Fuck, why don't I have my wiring diagram sitting in front of me? You have the CDI, or still on the old stator? The CDI I can walk you through no probs sight unseen, stator, I'll need a  little time to check the wiring diagrams.

Can you take a picture of your current wiring block and post it?

I just re-wired my magnum with the CDI, and it was simple as hell.
