So I finally got around to working on this some more. I wired in the external condensor all janky-like. That got it started, but it idled really unevenly and my points were still hosting what was apparently an awesome little fireworks party under my flywheel (and that condensor ran for over a month on my Free Spirit, so I know it's good). So then I pulled the stator plate off the parts Tomos, put new wires on it since the old ones were super brittle and gross, and put it on the blue Tomos. And now it runs. There's still a little arcing going on, and I don't know how much longer the old stock stuff will last, but it should be good long enough for me to order some more parts.
