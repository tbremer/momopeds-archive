Well now that I know just a wee smidgen more about mopeds, I'm learning how the Italians do mopeds. At first look it seems like some inpenetrable homogulation but I managed to get the engine dropped and torn down and I must say its quite impressive the way they designed this thing.

All of these things are also why the vespa is such a PITA to make serious upgrades too.
