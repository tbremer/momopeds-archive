Major progress made on the engine today! I got the spacers cut and roughed out enough to do a full engine mock-up including the TOMOS head. I ended up using some 2mm thick aluminum sheet I found at hobby lobby. Using two of these and three total base gaskets layered between the two spacers I ended up with 10.5mm total thickness, dead on what I needed to make it work.

The assembled result is just massive.....
