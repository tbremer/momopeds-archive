Treats has the Boss for $60.  Best pipe out there for the money.  It's what's on my magnum.  http://treatshq.com

Tires seem to be in short supply these days.  These are backordered until 10/24, but they're incredible tires: http://www.motorcycle-superstore.com/5/32/404/11893/ITEM/Michelin-Gazelle-M62-Moped-Tire.aspx?SiteID=CSE_GBase_404&WT.mc_ID=80003&zmam=88421133&zmas=1&zmac=2&zmap=11893

Chen Shins are ultimate cheap-o tires that don't last too long, but they're $14 each and better than old dry-rotted crap.  And they're like the only 17" tires in stock anywhere that aren't a total ripoff.  http://www.motorcycle-superstore.com/5/32/404/11580/ITEM/Cheng-Shin-C109-Moped-Tire.aspx

These are the tires I've got on most of my bikes, and it's a 2 tire / 2 tube deal from this site.  They're pretty damn good, but I honestly prefer the gazelles (but they're backordered).  http://www.mopedshop.com/2-Sava-moped-Tires-Tubes-p/savaspecial.htm
