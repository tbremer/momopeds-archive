Velosolex-America (Steve's Moped and Bicycle World) sells parts for the Mopex/ Blacknroll/ Velosolex S 4800. Most of these parts are made in China, but some are made elsewhere. For example, they sell Hutchinson tires which are made in France.

Mopedworld does not sell many Solex parts. They generally sell new parts from VeloCruz or good/used parts.
