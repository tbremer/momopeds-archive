Title: Urbie Express state of the union
Post by: FakeDad on September 13, 2010, 04:07:23 pm
Well it's been a few months since I've had her, here's what we've changed.

Cleaned carb- fixed the issue with gas building up in the bowl.
Lightened the variator- it's a six roller, took out 3 weights gave her a real power boost, but still maxed out at about 27mph.
Roasted pipe- un-dogged it, made her sound happier
New spark plug- no change
Dayton belt- definatly something I should have done sooner, reduced her out of the gate acceleration, but she hit 33 on my "test flats" greatly reduced strain on her, where she used to scream at 27, she's humming at 33.
Next step, 35 dollar expansion chamber.

I should probably fix that engine stud too, and she's running lean.
