Ok, I took another old problem/project over to Scooter today and ill give you all a little brake down. Ciao's have some of the coolest stock forks on the planet, but the get a little squirrelly at high speeds and in sharp turns. So i wanted to find a way to make them more rigid, more like a race fork feeling. So there was this one idea going around that involved little gas struts for hatch backs, but that required more movement then this was allowing for so i had an idea that required welding, and Mark said take the idea to Scooter, and so I did, Viola!! I came up with the idea to simpley remove the front tire and remove the fork springs then to skip the first piece of hardware and one coil then weld 5 coils.(As seen is pictures) This reduced the play by 40%. In other words, its going to be extremely rigid. If you find its not rigid enough you can just weld one more coil at a time until you find your desired stiffy ness.
