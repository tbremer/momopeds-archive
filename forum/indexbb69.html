Shit man, that sucks ass. Sorry, i thought we had it beaten. It's probably my fault for talking shit at your wheel afterwards.

Is your rubber strip intact inside? Take the tire ALL the way off and inspect inside I bet you have so wires pokiing or spokes protruding or somethinglike that. Popping tubes like that is pretty unusual unless something's poking them.

Is there a tiny hole in the tube, or more of a little tear?

You rode the parade with slightly flat tires, did you inflate more fully after the parade? If so, I would think there's something in your tire (like a wire) poking it when the pressure is higher.

You're probably gonna want new tires. No cash? Replace the one that's fucked up first, then next paycheck, get the other one going.

Local bike shops are WAY better than places with big, plastic displays and nothing but high-end shit. Find a Mom and Pop place for sure. Bicycle world in belleville was TOTALLY stoked just to help someone and make a few bucks.
