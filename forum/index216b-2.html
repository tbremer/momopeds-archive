Just an Idea, but I think its a good one...

Rotate the flywheel by hand and mark the position of rubbing on the flywheel in respect to the case.
remove flywheel
rotate crank by hand, if rubbing still occurs mark postion of Woodruff Key in respect to the case
re-install flywheel, see if rubbing on crank (flywheel removed) and rubbing on flywheel (flywheel installed) line up.

then you'd see if its the flywheel or the crank, or both.


Also! when fly wheel is removed roll it on a very flat (counter?) surface and see if the flywheel is warped.
