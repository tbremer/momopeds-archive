What, you don't want to ride with us this winter? We're just not cool enough? Fine, I see how it is.

But yeah. Don't winterize. Just ride it. There are enough good days here and there that you'll be able to ride. It's not like we live in Canada.

If you REALLY want to winterize it, though, I second the notion of putting gas in it. That's what I did before I left for Costa Rica. I just used unmixed gas so I wouldn't feel guilty about dumping it in my van when I got back, and then I stuck a big note on it like, "DON'T RUN THIS UNTIL YOU PUT PREMIX IN IT."
