The Christmas Party will be December 18th at 4 pm. Grilled checken and burgers are being provided. Please bring some sort of side dish or dessert. The gift exchange is as follows: Bring one wrapped moped related item. It can be used, but not used up. If you want or need to buy an item, limit it to $10 or less. Anyone who brings a gift will draw for a gift. It will be a random draw, so you won't be selecting a gift for a specific person.  After all the gifts are drawn for and unwrapped, there will be a swap. If you see something you like better than what you got, try to get the other person to trade. Make as many trades as you like. The main idea here is for everyone to get something they can use, so no gag gifts. The gift exchange is optional. See you there.
