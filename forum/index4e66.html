Oh, helps if I read beyond clubman...lolz

Suspension and shit will be fine.  I've ridden doubles on:
magnum with hydros many times
maxi without a double seat
moby 50v (moby couch seat FTW)
motron
garelli monza
probably more.

Things that get bad with two people:
brakes start to suck (just too much weight to slow down fast)
acceleration (just too much weight to get going fast)

Clutch might suffer if you're riding doubles all the time, but for the occasional ride, it'll be fine.
