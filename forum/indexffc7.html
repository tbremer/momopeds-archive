Local Discussion (MidMopeds) / Magnum update
« on: August 03, 2009, 01:49:46 pm »
I wanted to let everyone know how the magnum is doing since I have a completely backwards schedule from most everyone else and rarely get to wrench with y'all recently.  As most you know know, I have had the bike running for about 2 weeks now, and I have had pretty much no problems whatsoever, a true daily rider.  Takeoff is moped-like but a little revving before I let off the brakes and it pulls pretty well off the line.  But as soon as the pipe hits around 5-10 mph, the rpms drop like it just shifted and it MOVES...I am going to run at least another 2 tanks of 25:1 through it before I really open in up but I am pretty confident that it's doing low 40s right now, 50 here I come right? 

Anywhoo....The bike is an absolute dream to ride and if I may say so, looks like sex on wheels.  I'm not saying I am going to have sex with it, but I won't tell you I haven't contemplated the logistics.  I'm waiting until after break-in to drop money into some of the cosmetics (new controls, cleaning up the wiring, etc...)  but so far everything seems good.  Every plug chop I have done looks great.  I am getting a lot of greasy build-up around the exhaust but it seems normal for 25:1.  I'm am trying not to get too excited before it's totally dialed in but those of you that have seen me since it's been back on the road know how I feel.  A big thank you to EVERYONE who has helped with this.  I am sorry for being a lazy piece of shit with a non-running magnum on his porch for the last 4 months, please forgive me!!!

-b
