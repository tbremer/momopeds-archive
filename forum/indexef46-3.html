The Hard Seizing Of The K-Star: A Tragicomedy In Two Acts
ACT I
Jesse (our hero)has been blasting downhill at 50mph with the throttle wide open and locked his rear wheel.  He has been pushing it homeward for about a mile as he passes Ryan's (the villain) house . . .
Ryan: WTF!?
Jesse: I hard seized blasting downhill with the throttle wide open.
Ryan: (cackling menacingly) What kind of person does that!?
Jesse: Visionaries, Rock Stars, Adventurers, people of my ilk.
Ryan: I'll buy your MKII for seven dollars.
Jesse: Your offer offends me. However, I will allow you to assist me in investigating the damage.
Ryan: I accept!
The two pull the top off of the afflicted Moped and discover a particularly disastrous four point seize which has rendered the piston comically gnarly and the cylinder chipped and irretrievably unhappy. Evidence points to a combination of exhaust leakage and jackassery. The crank and rod seem to have escaped unscathed and are currently staying with relatives and would not return our calls.
Stay tuned for Act II in which our hero posts pictures and determines a course of action!
