Re: Do E50 stators fit on ZA50's
« Reply #6 on: January 19, 2010, 12:07:11 pm »
since it's an AC generator (basically) the thing causing the issue is going to be the flywheel timing lobe. the points on the ZA sit at the 2-oclock position (ish) and the points on an e50 are at the 9-oclock position and the flywheels are set up for that. so an e50 flywheel on a ZA crank wont line up correctly because the cam lobe is in the wrong position. (the woodruff key sets its position)

stator plate is interchangable, not so much the flywheel.
