What is the issue here with this NU?
« on: October 16, 2011, 02:44:27 pm »
OK!  Honda NU50, 60cc kit, high flow filter, bp4hs, port exhaust, 85 jet, stock carb, stock pipe :  Cant seem to find the issue maybe someone can give me ideas of where the problem may be, this is what it does:   It boggs out at 3/4-full throttle sometimes sooner.  Won't gain rpm's going downhill.  Plug reads are all ok no signs of lean or rich.  The 60cc kit is ported the same as the stock 49cc.  I've tried upjetting with no improvement and down to an 80 with no improvement!  Gas flow is good and the jets are clean. 

Things I've tried:  Make sure all is sealed, havent put new crank seals in but it all looks dry in those areas.  As said made sure fuel flow is good and getting where its supposed to, all is good!  I tried different new plugs and this one (bs4hs) seems to work the best (bpr5, bpr6 bp6hs) these plugs gave bad results in performance bogged out at 1/2 throttle the bp6hs was about the same as the bp4hs but the 4 overall was better.  I did put a regular car wire on from the coil to plug cause it didnt have a wire and that was hangin on the wall ready to use.  Normally it would take a resistor plug because of the resistor wire,, dont know if that may be an issue or not??  I opened up the exhaust port some but on the outer side no inner, so it should have no ill effects.  Tried different jetting up/down.. no help!  reeds are good!

The reason I know it will run better is because it has in the past.  I rode this at Holy Rollers BBQ last weekend and it ran as mentioned 28-30 tops  until halfway running on the river bottom I took the air filter off to see what it would do and it ran much better.  As we rode through old town.   it started running much better temps were coolong down.  By this time the bike was running as should not bogging at all and actually running great topping out at 35+.
  So the next day I took the air filter off expecting the same good results and it was acting again as mentioned shitty!! I still cant get it to run right no matter what I do with what I got.  Any help, ideas, advise, TNT would be appreciated thanks
