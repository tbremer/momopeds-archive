I think it would be fun to do a moped ally cat race and to my knowledge no one has ever had or hosted one before. 

What's an ally cat race? 
Ally cat races were started in New York and San Francisco as a way for bike messengers to prove who there skills and claim fame as fastest messenger in town. 

Here is what you do.
At the start everyone is given a map with 6 to 12 random locations on it and told to go!  You can go to them in any order and by whatever avenue you choose.  At each location you are given something or asked to do a random ridiculous task to prove that you were there.  Once you have gone to them all you race back to the start.   First one back wins bragging rights and awesome swag.
