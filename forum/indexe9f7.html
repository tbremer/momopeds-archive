So, as per the semi-planning discussion we had tonight:

I'm not comfortable taking my van to Austin -- it's broken down twice in the last 3 weeks. If it runs properly between now and then, and at the last minute we are left with no other choice, I will take it, but I'd rather not have to do that.

I know some of you were discussing rental vehicles. I don't think this is a feasible solution -- we can't afford mileage on a Uhaul, we can't tow a trailer with a rental vehicle, and we can't fit 10 mopeds into a rental van (or afford to have the gas smell removed afterwards). If someone can find a big Uhaul-like truck that we don't have to pay mileage on, then that might be the start of a plausible solution.

That leaves us with Brett's Jeep and Kenden's van. Ashley and Daryl are going in Ashley's car, and they can take 1 bike. That leaves me, Brett, Kenden, Spencer, Chris, Becca, Ed, and Trinity (if I'm forgetting anyone or if anyone had a change of heart, now's a good time to speak up) -- so 8 people, and 9 bikes, some of which are large bikes. Between those two vehicles, people space shouldn't be a problem. Moped space, though ... that will be tough. We need one trailer that can haul 9 mopeds, including a MB5 and Ed's Passport, assuming he's bringing it. If we split the gas for those two vehicles and one trailer rental (est. $100) between 8 people, it works out to about $110 per person. I think all of us would like to leave Thursday night, so Ed, is that doable for you? I know you said at one point that you don't get off until 2:30 in the morning or something. I'm pretty sure we'd all rather leave earlier than that, but we can talk and try to get something figured out.

At this point, anyone who drops will decrease the cost splitting without increasing efficiency, because if we have more than 3 people going, we're going to need both vehicles. So please guys, if you want out, say so now before things get any more finalized.
