you should retard you timing just a touch. the book calls for between 1.30 to 1.70 mm before top dead center.

again I would mark the flywheel, and the case, then check it while running with a timing lite.

when I checked mine with the lite it strobed once every 180 deg. until you gave it a little gas (at which point the mark would only show up on the opposite side of the flywheel)

the box never advanced the timing (auto magically), in fact treats web site makes no mention of automatic advance for the box he sells. (I don't know where I got the idea that this thing had "auto advance")
