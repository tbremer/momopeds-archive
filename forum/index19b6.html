well then thats not much incentive for us to help you. 

From what you have described it just sounds like the clutch was put back together wrong.  Most likely when everything is tightened down the composite clutch plate is a little off center or is being pinched by the two outer metal plates.  Do you have the tiny metal sliver that goes on one of the teeth of the composite plate.  That actually keeps it centered.  If its not there that might explain your problem.
