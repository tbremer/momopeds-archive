Repair and Performance / Re: Paint and such
« on: September 17, 2009, 11:42:32 am »
Industrial paint stripper. 3 or 4 coats usually pulls the thick paint off wheels. Get it down to metal, clean it, sand it, sand it some more..then sand it some more. Go gradually to finer grit till you hit scotchbright pads. Clean it again, take it to a sterile area and hang it up, paint it. Make sure the humidity isnt too high and the temp is not to low or high.

I worked at a body shop for a summer, painted a few cars. The best way to do it, is to have a carport sized ventilated room for paint. Bake it on.
