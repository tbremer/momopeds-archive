Wait, from under where it says Bing? If where I think you mean is where you mean (the area where the carb attaches to the intake), I'd be inclined to say you've got an air leak. Bings are notorious for leaking there, so it wouldn't surprise me if yours was. I think it's time for the carb cleaner test:

//www.mopedarmy.com/wiki/Air_leak

And if you fail the carb cleaner test, go to the hardware store and get an O-ring that will fit inside the mouth of the carb where it attaches to the intake. Then smush the carb down really hard onto the intake (to compress the O-ring), then tighten the F out of the clamp screw. Then re-try the carb cleaner test and see if you've actually improved it.
