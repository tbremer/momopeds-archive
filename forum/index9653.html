Title: Re: 4 stroking puch
Post by: Moped Whisperer on June 11, 2013, 07:23:29 pm
If it is four-stroking, making it more rich is the exact opposite of what you want to do. if it was running fine and suddenly stopped, something is wrong outside of tuning. Have you done anything else to it like a new air filter or jammed something into your pipe?s stinger?    A smaller jet or less restrictive filter will lean you out and get rid of four-stroking but if your problem is something other than just your carbs jetting, you run the risk of seizing at lower rpms.
