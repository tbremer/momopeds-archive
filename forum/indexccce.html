ALOW1 you ever messed with one of these or heard/know andthing aobut them?
No I have never messed with one. I have never even seen one in person. The picture of it reminds me of the newer C3 Yamaha's that are out now. Also kind of reminds me of the early 60's Cushman's.

As for Yamaha scoots, I have owned 2 92+ Jogs and an 01 Vino. All of them had Horizontal Minarelli's in them. The Jog and Vino motors are the same except the Vino motors have a larger intake transfers.

As much as I love my Honda's, it is fact that the Minarelli's in the Jog's can run faster than the Dio's. I have seen people bolt the 92+ Jog motors into the Razz frames. If this motor is the same as the Razz motor is as far as bolting up, then maybe you can stick a Jog motor in it.

That is if its something your going to keep. If you just want to play with it, then I would go with Kenden's original plan of a 19mm carb, a good port job and make a pipe to fit it.
