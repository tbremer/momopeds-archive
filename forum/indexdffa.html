Oh...but the real question, regardless of 1977mopeds....

I wouldn't buy that thing.  It's too expensive, and most likely real cheaply made.  I worked at a chinese scooter shop for a while, and really don't trust anything imported from there.  They make the engines from powdered metal.  Yes, they put powdered aluminum in a press rather than pouring molten material into a mold.  This is why the stuff breaks all the time.  On top of that there's the cheap electronics, the fact that it's 4 stroke, the impossibility of tuning the thing to be faster...  Blech
