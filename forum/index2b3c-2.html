Repair and Performance / Pinto/Aprilia project. Now with VIDEO!
« on: March 20, 2010, 07:35:05 pm »
Pinto/Aprilia hybrid that I posted on the project thread. I figured I'll just make a post for that bike.
O.G race pinto with disc brakes and hydraulic forks.
aprilia scarabeo motor/trans with 16" mags a la Nate Bandit.
50cc aircooled two stroke, dual variated. stock cylinder,
carb undecided, pipe undecided.

first, had to cut the swing arm mount off, the motor mounts up just like a hobbit, except it uses bearings, not bushings in the case. Used the factory holes for alignment of the new engine mounts, only drilled out to accept 1 inch o.d tubing.
Had to center the new rim in the forks and move the caliper over to match. Then used some straight 1x1's to align the front wheel to the rear wheel. Still have to make some engine mount brackets out of flat stock and get it welded up. This should be a really fun bike. I'll continue this when I mod the shit out of the motor.
