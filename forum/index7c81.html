Title: Re: Puck brake question
Post by: Phil N. on June 27, 2011, 02:13:06 pm
I'd avoid lube near your pads.  Maybe sand them down a little so they don't rub.  Also is your break cable too tight that it is making the pads rub?  Whatever you do make sure to clean the shit outta the hubs breaks after you sand them.  Install the breaks with clean hands too.

Oh I was just thinking it wouldn't hurt to check your axl to make sure it's not bent, too.
