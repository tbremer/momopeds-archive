Take your pick of places to work.  While not as desirable as Chem or Physics, you should still get some pretty decent leads.  Don't go with some crappy job though as you'll be able to get something in a better district or school with that degree.  This is a very, very hard time to get a teaching job though.  I'm certified in HS Earth Science-astronomy and middle school science and I'm struggling to even get interviews at this point.  I have a Master's though and I think I'm viewed as too expensive for many districts in this time of budget cutting so that will give you a small leg up on some applicants.  Congratulations.  Teaching is one of the best jobs in the world.
