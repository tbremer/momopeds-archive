Broke both my legs in accident with car.
« on: October 13, 2009, 03:57:37 pm »
I was involved in an accident on my PX on Saturday morning.
Happened @ 9am @ Clayton and Yale in St. Louis, MO. Car pulled out in
front of me to turn left. I wanted to go around her, but if i went in front
of her, she'd hit me. And I couldn't go behind her b/c there was no room
cuz she'd had barely/hadn't cleared the curb yet. And if I was to slam on
the brakes hard enough to maybe stop in time, I'd figured I'd wipe out and
go underneath her car. She was moving pretty slowly across the lane so by
the time hit her, she was across both lanes of traffic. PX is totalled, of
course. I flew over her car and landed on the ground; tried too move my
legs out of the awkward position they were in and realized I couldn't move
my legs. Final prognosis is both legs broken at the femur, as well as a bone broken in the left hand. (which is gonna affect using crutches or a wheelchair
until the hand heals) Docs say I should be walking by myself in 3 months,
and should be back to normal in 6 months. Surprisingly, they didn't put
casts on; instead they shoved titanium rods in my legs to stabilize and
strengthen the fractures. Can't work @ JJ's any more, but the Library shuffled some things around so I'll still have a job whenever I am ready. Hopefully I can still do school. I'm @ Barnes till tomorrow, room 7486, , if you wanna call the number here is 362.5894. So yeah. This sucks. But I'll ride again.
