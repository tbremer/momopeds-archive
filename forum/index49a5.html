So I got a wild hair up my ass last night and put on the expansion pipe.  Just chopped the old pipe off at the header, and welded on a new one.  Of course the kickstand is in the way, so I will cut off one side and bend the other for a side stand.   I also took the extra cylinder I have and started opening up the intake.  They are friggin tiny!   I will make a new intake for the carb and see how much power I get from just that little bit of work.  With just the new pipe I got enough low end to climb my big ass hill in front of the house.  I used to have to pedal or use my feet like flinstones to get up at all. 

These little 50cc are so much work compared to the electrics!  Of course none of my electrics look as cool as the Golden Bullet.  This thing is CLASSIC!
