Yet another Moby for sale
« on: May 13, 2013, 04:28:21 pm »
Howdy Ya'll. I'm looking to get rid of a Moby 50v/LeMoped hybrid that I've had for a couple of years. I bought the bike(s) from Shaner and pieced them together with the help of Phil Norath. It's a runner, but not my most favorite ride or one that I have time to fuss with.

The engine was ported by Phil and you know that cat did a good job. It blows for low end, but once it gets up there it's decent. It does hate hills and will need some fine tuning.

The frame comes from a cherry red 50'v, complete with cherry red side covers and toolbox covers. There are Gold mags on there with fresh tires and tubes, couldn't be more than 50 miles on em.

The engine did come from the LeMoped and has the four rollerweight variator. The starter clutch has been swiss cheesed my Sam N. to make it lighter. Works great. Two roller weights have been removed from the variator, but I've saved the other balls and have lots of extra parts including another engine that needs work...Comes with the classic dellorto 14.12 and intake for it.

All new break cables and such.

The front forks come from a hobbit and the front tire is not perfectly centered in it. It rides a lil squirrely and may be something getting used to. It's got the long LeMoped seat as well.

I dunno, I put a shit ton of time into this thing and rather not deal with it anymore. NOT expecting what I put into it monitarily. I will post pics soon. Wanted to put it out there and see who is interested.

I'm thinking 350 for the whole thing. It will need a little bit of love from someone, but could definitely be a badass runner if you put some time into it.

PM and we can figure it out. Also, I live in Columbia. Hope the moped world is treating ya'll right.

Taylor
