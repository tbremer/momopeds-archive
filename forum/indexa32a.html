running sluggishly for a half mile,,could be  ,low tires,, dragging brakes,,, dirt in the carb,,, slipped timing,, slipped points,,, or perhaps soft seize.,,,,
back firing when trying to start it,, could be flooded motor,,,dirt in the carb,,, slipped timing,, slipped points,,,lost compression,,,from a soft seize
Start with the easiest thing,, and work your way to the hardest,,,,
 squeeze the tires,, spin them freely,, take out the plug, see if its wet,,, pull the hose to the carb,, see if the fuel flows freely, look thru the little window in the flywheel, and see if the points are opening,,and note where the stator plate is set,, do a compression test,take the carb off and clean it,,,, remove the cylinder,,, clean aluminum off cylinder,,, order new piston,,, every time you get stuck,, trying to figure out what to do next,,, clean the bike,, and organize your tools,,,
