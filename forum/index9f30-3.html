one for the books
« on: June 07, 2010, 11:30:04 am »
So last night me, squints, and sidecovers, met up at sidecovers place and watched the second half of weird science, got bored, and squints took us on a really nice ride south o town around grant road. NO traffic, perfect temps, squints as leadin and blastin, and i was grinnin the whole time. Hit the top of the garage for some wheelies and jumps, a quick smoke, then to shakes to wind down a bit.

2 bigass greyhounds later, we got brave and hopped back on our bikes and rode the KT Trail all the way out to Rocheport without regard for gas, tools, water, the illegality of it, or the darkness and unpredictability of the woods. I had a pedal fall off about a mile into the trail, and sidecovers actually did too a few miles later. Smoked again under the 70 overpass. it was creepy. For what it's worth, the trail IS pretty nice to ride on. I think we were all goin close to 30! My rear wheel slipped in some extra gravely spots a few times, but we made it with no probs. Lightning bugs swarmed.

From Rocheport we busted out 240 to 40, rollin with no lights under the moon, and hit Midway for some breakfast and coffee. Came out and we were all WAYYYY low on gas. We were all grateful not to have run out. Gallon and a half and some cheapo oil later we were back in the saddle, headin home. Made it back at i have no idea what time, discussed the relevant safety issues, and parted ways. We weren't passed by a single car all night.

good ridin y'alls!
