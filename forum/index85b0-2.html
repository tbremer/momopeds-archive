Title: Re: Cotter pin and pedal arm removal
Post by: Ryan N. on August 23, 2013, 11:53:20 pm
this is what I do, back the nut off until it is at the very tip but flush with the tip of the pin.  hit it with a mallet until its sitting flush on the pedal arm.  that way you don't damage the threads.  back the nut off a turn or two and whack it again, at this point you can take the nut off and put a phillips or a punch on the pin and hit it with a hammer and it should pop out.  if it's real stubborn you may need to let it soak in wd-40 or some sort of lubricating oil.
