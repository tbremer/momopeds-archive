Title: HD museum not SO bad.
Post by: stupedcat ca/ on April 01, 2012, 09:11:47 am
i went to the barfley davidson museum a coupla weeks ago with my family. i had REALLY low expectations and expected to see a bunch of shitty faux choppers and fat weekend warrior/losers.

turns out, not THAT BAD. you could take pictures. they were having speical displays of all black mc gangs which were really cool.

turns out the first harleys were belt driven mopeds. i do not know why i didn't take pics of those...i wasn't even high. they did have recordings of the engines.


my favorite were the board racers. no brakes or starter clutches and hand pumped oil injectors. RACE TILL YOU RUN OUTTA GASS! i guess they used to have a thunderdrome in kc. the videos of them racing were SICK.

that awesome stand up sidecar was on a indian. dunno why that was there.

all in all it wasn't as bad as i thought. we went at a good time so that there wasn't tons of shit hawks there. i really love the early pre ww2 bikes.
