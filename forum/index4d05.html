For the last month, I've been working as an assistant mechanic over at Maplewood Scooter Company. Since I'll be starting my new teaching job next month, they're gonna need a replacement. COULD IT BE YOU!?!?

Your job will be mostly oil changes, tire changes, carb cleans, dealer preps, and other miscellaneous wrench work. The more you already know how to do, the better! They are located at Big Bend and Manchester Rd. You can go apply in person, and/or ask me any questions you may have.
