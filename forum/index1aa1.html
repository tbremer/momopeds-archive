Unwrapped two skids last night containing a 1999 euro ciao w/ 17" twisty mags-made in spain (what?), a 1995 euro bravo with long seat and 4 spoke mags, a minty Safari 400MT Turbo-case inducted, fancooled, kitted, pipe,carb, cdi (pretty peppy bike right out of the box), and a Safari C2.  The c2 is mine the MT is my cousins and one vespa for each of us.

I am sure my cousin will post pics of the MT soon, it is perty.  I will post pics of everything else tonight.
