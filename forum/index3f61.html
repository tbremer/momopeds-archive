derbi 6 rollers are not easy to find.  they also do not match the "ramp" of the hobbit clutch bell BUT I did successfully use one on a hobbit athena build and it was KILLER and I bet would be even MORE killer had I machined the face of the variator and clutch bell to match.

the tjt is great but also needs to be machined for the proper angle (it is meant for a derbi after all) but most people do not machine them and they run great, so it's whatever...

if you have access to a derbi 6 roller i'd say do that --- the ramp plate is far less aggressive than the tjt ... the tjt kinda just SLAMS you into the "gear" ... if you have one you know what I mean.
