Title: Free VIN check
Post by: JCalton on June 21, 2013, 12:36:24 pm
For what it's worth, you can check vin numbers for free 3 times a day to see if they've been stolen (or totalled). This works for motorcycles to the extent that they are reported.
//www.nicb.org/theft_and_fraud_awareness/vincheck

Just make sure if you are paying for an actual NMVITS  (//www.vehiclehistory.gov/nmvtis_vehiclehistory.html)that you pay like $5 not $25 (i.e., don't click on the NICB's sponsored link).
