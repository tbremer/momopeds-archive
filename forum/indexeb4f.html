General Discussion / POR-15 is some badass shit
« on: October 25, 2010, 07:05:46 pm »
So I'm cooking with POR-15 in the kitchen trying to get my Honda Aero tank (That I fucking thought was fine) to stop leaking.

I cleaned it out and despite being relatively thick there were several pin holes in the bottom of it...  no wonder it wouldn't hold gas.

So I got the POR-15 starter kit that includes the "Wash" the "Etcher" and the actual por-15, and all I can say for sure is that my kitchen sink is washed and etched!  That shit found every loose spot on the bottom of the tank and ate right through it.  Crazy thing was, the leak almost stopped by the time it had been in there 15 minutes. 

Waiting on the tank to dry and then the actual por 15 is going in.  If that doesn't seal the holes...   well i'll tie a milk jug to the side and plumb that in or something.

Oh, and the back wheel of the Aero was acting up - so i wrenched on it and got it turning just as it should be.  As soon as this tank is done I'll be riding at like... 35mph... almost double the speed of the Spree.
