A summary:

After donning cheesy sweaters, getting gas, and taking Mike back to get his Vespa (since the Pinto developed a lack-of-compression problem), we were on our way. Brett's Bat was going at least as fast as my Slowmos, which pissed me the hell off (stupid Tomos), although I was one of the few people who made it up the hill at the overlook without pedaling (yay Tomos!).

Oh, and there were cheesy sweaters. Lots of cheesy sweaters. Some woman at the pumpkin patch said mine was the best... I'm not sure if that's a good thing though, especially since everyone else had to go to the Salvation Army to get theirs, and mine came from my mom's closet... Also, I wore a cape.
