For proper penetration, you should always estimate about 1 amp per .001" of steel thickness.  .1" steel needs 100 amps, which is about 10 amps going through a 110v line before the welder does it's conversion.  On the 220 it is only 5 amps. 


I installed a breaker just for my 220v welder too.  Can you weld near the breaker box?  Installing a welder plug on the wall to code really isn't too hard.
