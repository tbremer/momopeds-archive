You'll never damage the quality converting to TIFF or PNG because they are lossless.  I'm not sure I would worry about converting JPEGs that you already have into PNG unless you are editing them.  They are already in their final format.  I'd just leave them be unless you want to clean them up (scratches, artifacts, color correction).  Then I would convert to PNG and leave them in that format.

So for cleaning up things that have already been PDF'd by someone else I think you are on the right track.  PDF>PNG>Edit>PDF

For things that you are scanning yourself you can convert those TIFFs to PNG or you can go straight from TIFF to PDF. It won't matter.

What settings do you use when scanning?  Do you do any OCR?
