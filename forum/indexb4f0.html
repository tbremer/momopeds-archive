Here is a pic of the bike.  It might look like it's being worked on but I have done hardly anything with it since I got it a year ago.  Chrome looks good but some rust on the engine and exhaust that can probably be sanded off.  I put a new battery in it when I got it as well as a new key cylinder and the dashboard lit up.  The transmission shifts cleanly and would indicate neutral on the dash as well.  The bike is relatively complete from what I can tell including a nifty backup tank that looks like a canteen and straps to the side. When I got the bike it turned over fine.  I got as far as being able to get a test light to flicker at the spark plug boot but I could never get a spark at the plug.  This was probably last April.  The bike sat until November and when I tried to kick it, it was frozen up.  I haven't touched it since.  It didn't seize while running so it shouldn't be too bad.  If someone is actually interested in it though, I'll just go ahead and try to take the cylinder off so I can give an idea what's going on.  Maybe a jammed up kickstart too, I haven't checked it out...
It could definitely be delivered when we are up for the rally
