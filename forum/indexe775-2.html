borrow a truck or van and bring it over tomorrow to 2905 texas ave, 63118, around 5ish... adjusting the chain is so easy, even I can help you there.

you put type F ATF in the plastic screw hole near where it says sachs on the clutch cover (the squarish part, not the shiny, round part). add just enough so it doesn't dribble out when it is standing flat on the kickstand... you can do homework here: http://www.mopedarmy.com/wiki/Category:Sachs

get it sorted out tomorrow, and you can have a worry-free time this weekend!
