I delete the two posts from BestBikeEngine.com.  Reason being: I can't stand spammers.  I deal with them multiple times daily on this and other sites I run/work on.  They are detrimental to online communities, and only out to make a buck and raise their google rating.

Reasons for deleting their posts:
1 - It's spam
2 - The place most likely sells cheap chinese engines - I worked at Popcycle, I know what that means
3 - It is nothing to do with our group or St. Louis
4 - I actually responded to one post, then noticed they posted the same thing in more than one of our forums, which is lame and annoying, so then I deleted them both.

Anyways, I know some people do like the helper engines that go on bicycles and such, so I thought I'd post here saying why I deleted the posts from them, but still let you know about the site.

I'm still feeling out my position as moderator here, so yell at me if you don't think I should have deleted the posts.
