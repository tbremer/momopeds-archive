Title: Re: no more "fix it" gatherings?
Post by: Ryan N. on June 21, 2013, 09:45:10 pm
digital caliper with a depth gauge ... put it down the spark plug hole ... set at 0mm when you are at TDC and then take it away from the direction your motor spins (so you would spin the motor counter clockwise to get BTDC on an e50) to get BTDC, make marks on flywheel... timing light.
