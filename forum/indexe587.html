Repair and Performance / Me points man
« on: August 18, 2009, 04:52:26 pm »
So the 2 speed maxi has been acting up, let my friend ride it and we went for a pretty long ride. It died on him and he thought it was out of gas. Well we loaded it on the truck and took it home. I put gas in it this morning and rode it to the bank. It died on me, sounds like a loss of spark to me. So I walk it home and get it started again, runs great for a little while till it heats up, then dies again. I sprayed for air leaks, none to be found. I started rev-ing it up and I watched the points open and close. There is a small spark every once in a while. I cleaned the points with a piece of sandpaper and now it wont start at all. I was thinking I need to set the correct points gap. I also have it at just a little before full advance, when it was at full advance it would get hot and soft sieze on me. Doesnt soft sieze anymore but now I gotta figure out the electrical issues. Anyone have a clue, or has this happend to you before... remember 2 speeds go the opposite direction as 1 speeds, but if you had this problem on a 1 speed maybe it would be the same issue on a 2 speed. My first idea was that the condenser has the problem, but this bike is immaculate and the stator looks like new, I guess thats not really the best judge of things but I'm kinda scratchin  my head and would like to ask questions before I start tearing it apart and over-work myself.
