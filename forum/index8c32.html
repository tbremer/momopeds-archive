Title: Re: Learn how to make handy things
Post by: madmatmax on January 08, 2012, 05:28:28 pm
I started a little foundry. I took an old smoker and flipped it upsidedown. I welded a 3'' pipe to a slotted brake disc and set that inside a cut down 5 gallon bucket for the inner chamber. I made a huge batch of this mixture of refractory: //www.backyardmetalcasting.com/refractories.html and cast it around the 5 gallon bucket inside the smoker. Then let it cure, knocked the 3'' pipe/ brake disc back through, and cut the bucket out. It is real low buck. I was going to fire it with hard wood charcoal. Somehow I haven't ever tried it out yet.
